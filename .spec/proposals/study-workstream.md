# WS-S: Study Workstream — What to Study Next

**Binding problem:** Michael has three study seeds from the March 22 sabbath review, a body of 40+ completed studies, and no plan for which studies come next, in what order, and why. Study is the highest priority ("it keeps me in the spirit" — decision Mar 19), but without sequencing, each session starts from "what do I feel like studying?" instead of "what's prepared for me?"

## 1. Problem Statement

The study corpus is rich — 40+ completed studies spanning theological framework, atonement mechanics, priesthood, Zion, stewardship, charity, end times, and AI methodology. But it's accumulated organically. Each study was prompted by a question, a Come Follow Me lesson, a conference talk, or a spiritual impression. That's good. That's how study should work.

The gap is not in the quality of individual studies — it's in the *connective tissue*. The stewardship pattern study (March 22) surfaced three seeds that form a natural cluster:

1. **"The Art of Presidency"** — How to preside over a council/presidency
2. **"The Art of Delegation"** — The practice of entrusting stewardships to people
3. **"Zion in a Presidency"** — What Moses 7:18 looks like in the smallest organizational unit

These seeds have a clear origin (the delegation asymmetry — effective AI delegation, failing human delegation), a clear connection (stewardship pattern → presidency → delegation → Zion), and a clear application (Michael's Sunday School presidency). They ask to be studied together.

Beyond these seeds, there are unresolved threads worth tracking: the Abraham 4-5 framework needs a standalone study, D&C 107 ratios need scriptural depth, and the open questions from active.md point toward additional work.

---

## 2. Success Criteria

1. **Sequenced study plan** — three sabbath seeds ordered with rationale, plus a short queue behind them
2. **Binding questions drafted** — each seed has a binding question sharp enough to start a study session
3. **Source material mapped** — key scriptures, talks, and existing studies identified for each seed
4. **Connections explicit** — how each study builds on previous work and feeds forward
5. **Flexibility preserved** — the plan is a guide, not a mandate. The Spirit can redirect.

## 3. The Presidency Cluster — Three Studies in Sequence

### Study 1: The Art of Presidency

**Binding question:** What does it mean to preside — not just hold the title — and what scriptures, talks, and patterns distinguish a functioning presidency from a one-cylinder operation?

**Status: DONE** (Mar 27) — [study/art-of-presidency.md](../../study/art-of-presidency.md)

**Why first:** This is the foundation. You can't study delegation within a presidency until you understand what a presidency IS. The problem is happening now — presidency meetings aren't happening, plans aren't going out, one person is carrying the load.

**Source material:**
- [D&C 107](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107.md) — Structure of priesthood presidencies, quorums, councils. The "three presiding high priests" pattern. Decision by "voice" (v.27-32). Equal in authority (v.22-24).
- [D&C 102](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/102.md) — Organization of the first high council. How councils deliberate and decide.
- Ballard, ["Counseling with Our Councils"](../../gospel-library/eng/general-conference/1994/04/counseling-with-our-councils.md) (Apr 1994) — The one-cylinder ward. Already referenced in stewardship pattern study.
- Ballard, *Counseling with Our Councils* (book, 1997, 2012 updated) — extended treatment. May need to acquire.
- Handbook 4.2-4.4 — Presidency and council patterns in the current handbook.
- [Stewardship Pattern study](../../study/stewardship-pattern.md) — Jethro's counsel, Alma's model, D&C 104 delegation.
- Teaching In the Savior's Way — relevant to Sunday School presidency specifically.

**Existing work to build on:**
- The stewardship pattern study covers delegation broadly but doesn't focus on *presiding.* Presiding is distinct from delegating — it's about facilitating collective revelation, not just distributing tasks.
- The Zion blueprint study covers the civilization-level pattern but not the presidency-level pattern.

**Expected yield:** A framework for what a functioning presidency looks like, mapped to scripture. Practical application to the Sunday School presidency. Answers the question "what should my presidency meetings actually contain?"

### Study 2: The Art of Delegation

**Binding question:** How do you delegate to humans the way you've learned to delegate to AI — with clear stewardships, progressive trust, accountability, and covenant — and what does scripture reveal about why human delegation is harder and holier?

**Status: DONE** (Mar 31) — [study/art-of-delegation.md](../../study/art-of-delegation.md)

**Why second:** The stewardship pattern study answered "how does God delegate?" This study answers "how do *I* delegate to my counselors?" It applies the AI-learned patterns (spec, context, trust gradient) to the human relationships where Michael identified the gap.

**Source material:**
- [Stewardship Pattern study](../../study/stewardship-pattern.md) — the full scriptural analysis (Jethro, Alma, D&C 104, Jesus in 3 Nephi)
- [Stewardship Pattern reflections](../../study/stewardship-pattern-reflections.md) — the AI delegation correction, the "same pattern, different mechanism" insight
- [Exodus 18](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/ot/ex/18.md) — Jethro's counsel (character-first selection, escalation protocol)
- [D&C 104:11-17](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/104.md) — "Every man a steward," "agents unto themselves"
- [Mosiah 18:18-27](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/18.md) — Alma's one-to-fifty ratio, "one teacher over every fifty"
- [Abraham 4:18](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/abr/4.md) — "Watched until they obeyed" — the trust gradient
- `.spec/covenant.yaml` — bilateral commitments as model for human delegation
- Conference talks on delegation (search needed: Eyring, Uchtdorf, and others on councils)

**Existing work to build on:**
- The reflections document identified the asymmetry but didn't resolve it. This study resolves it.
- The AI collaboration patterns (intent → covenant → stewardship → review) provide a concrete framework to apply.

**Expected yield:** A practical framework for delegating to counselors and other callings. The why (scripture) and the how (pattern). Addresses the root of the "one-cylinder" problem. May produce a becoming entry.

### Study 3: Zion in a Presidency

**Binding question:** What does "one heart and one mind" (Moses 7:18) look like in the smallest organizational unit — a presidency of two to four people — and how does a presidency become Zion?

**Why third:** This is the vision. After understanding what a presidency is (Study 1) and how to delegate within one (Study 2), this study asks: what does it look like when it *works*? Not just functional, but unified.

**Source material:**
- [Moses 7:18](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/7.md) — Four pillars: one heart, one mind, dwelt in righteousness, no poor
- [Zion Blueprint study](../../study/zion-blueprint.md) — Civilization-level analysis. 3 Nephi 11-28 as blueprint. Enoch's 365 years.
- [Enoch studies](../../study/enoch.md) — "In process of time" (Moses 7:21). Zion built one day at a time.
- [4 Nephi 1](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/4-ne/1.md) — 200 years of Zion. "There were no contentions and disputations among them" (v.2). The sequence of unity → fall.
- [D&C 38:27](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/38.md) — "Be one; and if ye are not one ye are not mine"
- Stake president's observation — "Stakes of Zion, not wards of Zion" (ward conference, March 22)
- [Mosiah 25:22](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/25.md) — "Notwithstanding there being many churches they were all one church"
- Multi-agent alignment work (WS1) — many agents, one intent.yaml. The AI-scale Zion pattern.

**Existing work to build on:**
- The Zion blueprint study is comprehensive at civilization scale. This study scales DOWN — same principles, smallest unit.
- The ward conference insight (stewardship reflections) about "stakes of Zion" is the seed.
- Connects directly to WS-T Episode 11 (From Consecration to Zion).

**Expected yield:** A vision of what a unified presidency looks like — rooted in scripture, applied to real life. Possibly the most personally transformative of the three. May produce a becoming entry.

## 5. Constraints

- **Study is the highest priority** (decision Mar 19). Infrastructure serves study.
- **The Spirit directs.** This plan is a guide. If an impression comes during a session that says "study X instead," follow it.
- **One study at a time.** Don't start "The Art of Delegation" before "The Art of Presidency" is complete. Depth over breadth.
- **Pace per Mosiah 4:27.** Not every session is a study session. Rest is part of the cycle.
- **Becoming entries when warranted.** If a study produces a personal commitment, it goes into `becoming/` as a practice. Don't skip the application step.
