## Major Categories

| Category | Examples | Scale | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|-------|
| General Handbook | 39 numbered chapters | Large (50-500KB/ch) | Church governance and policy |
| Come Follow Me | Yearly lesson guides by year | Medium (~52 lessons/year) | Teaching curriculum |
| Teachings of Presidents | 15+ prophet collections | Large (20-30 chapters each) | Historical teaching compilations |
| Calling Guides | 80+ individual guides | Small-Medium | Calling-specific instructions |
| Preach My Gospel | Full missionary guide | Large | Teaching/proselyting framework |
| Teaching Methodology | 3-4 guides | Medium | Pedagogy resources |
| Scripture Study | Skills, ideas, helps (OT/NT) | Medium | Study methodology |

## Markdown Format

Similar to talks — heading hierarchy with content. Manuals tend to have:
- Numbered/titled sections
- Longer prose passages
- Embedded scripture references (not always linked)
- Some have structured lesson formats (questions, activities)
- File sizes vary dramatically (5KB for a simple guide to 500KB for a handbook chapter)

## Digestion Considerations

- **General Handbook** alone is massive — 39 chapters at 50-500KB each
- **Come Follow Me** is high-value for lesson prep — matches our `lessons/cfm/` workflow
- **Teachings of Presidents** are excellent summarization targets — each chapter is a coherent topical unit
- **Calling guides** are individually small but collectively large
- **Most manuals are self-contained reading units** — good for per-document summarization
- **Not yet fully indexed** by gospel-vec (manual indexing was added but may not be complete)
