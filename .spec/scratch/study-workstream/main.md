## Sabbath Seeds (March 22)

Three studies born from the delegation irony — effective at delegating to AI, failing at delegating to human councilors:

### 1. "The Art of Presidency"
- How to preside over a council/presidency
- Not just "be the boss" — the priesthood model of presiding
- Ballard's "Counseling with Our Councils" is anchor talk
- D&C 107 presidency structure
- Practical: Michael is Sunday School president with two counselors and a secretary
- The presidency meetings aren't happening. Plans aren't being made. Assignments aren't going out.
- Connection to stewardship-pattern.md — Jethro's counsel, Alma's model

### 2. "The Art of Delegation"
- Deeper than the stewardship pattern study (which covered HOW God delegates)
- This is the PRACTICE — how Michael delegates to his human councilors
- Applying the AI delegation patterns (clear spec, progressive trust, covenant) to human relationships
- The reflection in stewardship-pattern-reflections.md identified the asymmetry but didn't resolve it
- Connection to WS-T teaching: you can't teach delegation if you haven't learned it

### 3. "Zion in a Presidency"
- Moses 7:18 applied to the smallest organizational unit (2-4 people)
- Building on zion-blueprint.md (civilization-level) but scaling DOWN
- "One heart and one mind" in a presidency that meets weekly
- The stake president's observation: "stakes of Zion" not "wards of Zion" — but what about presidencies?
- Connection to multi-agent alignment (WS-T Episode 11) — the presidency IS a multi-agent system
