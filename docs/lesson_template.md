## Preparation

### Personal Spiritual Preparation
Before preparing the lesson content, prepare yourself spiritually:
- [ ] Pray for guidance and for those you will teach
- [ ] Study the assigned material personally
- [ ] Ponder how the principles have blessed your life
- [ ] Seek the Spirit's guidance on what to emphasize

### Know Your Learners
Consider the specific needs, circumstances, and strengths of those you teach:
- What challenges might class members be facing?
- What questions might they have about this topic?
- How can you help them feel the Spirit?

## Teaching Principles Checklist

Based on [Teaching in the Savior's Way](../gospel-library/eng/manual/teaching-in-the-saviors-way-2022/01-introduction/03a-overview-of-christlike-teaching.md):

### Love Those You Teach
- [ ] Prayed for class members by name
- [ ] Plan to create a safe environment for sharing
- [ ] Prepared to show genuine interest in their contributions

### Teach by the Spirit
- [ ] Prepared myself spiritually
- [ ] Willing to adjust lesson based on spiritual promptings
- [ ] Plan to bear testimony

### Teach the Doctrine
- [ ] Focus on scriptures and words of latter-day prophets
- [ ] Doctrine leads to conversion and builds faith in Christ
- [ ] Help learners find personal relevance

### Invite Diligent Learning
- [ ] Questions encourage learners to search and discover
- [ ] Opportunities for learners to share insights
- [ ] Clear invitation to live what they're learning
