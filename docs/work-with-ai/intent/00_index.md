# Intent-Driven Development Research

**Started:** February 2026
**Status:** Active research
**Core question:** How should we specify intent for AI agents — solo and at team scale — and what gospel patterns illuminate (or go beyond) what the industry is discovering?

---

## Why This Research Exists

> "I want to learn how to use artificial intelligence like God uses real intelligence. And I want to glorify others with that knowledge, light, and truth, lifting them up and empowering them to accomplish great things."

This directory is a structured exploration of how the software industry is converging on **specification-driven development** — and how the patterns God established long before silicon existed can guide us further than the industry has gone on its own.

## Key Themes Emerging

1. **Spec quality is THE bottleneck** — replacing implementation speed across the entire industry
2. **File-based > tool-internal state** — specs must live in the repo, travel with the code, be version-controlled
3. **Solo and team are the same pattern at different scales** — the spec-driven workflow works for one person or fifty
4. **The industry is converging on what the gospel already teaches** — intent before creation, spiritual before temporal, stewardship with accountability
5. **There are gospel patterns the industry hasn't discovered yet** — see [03_beyond-intent.md](03_beyond-intent.md)
