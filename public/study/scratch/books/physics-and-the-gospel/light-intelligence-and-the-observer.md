# Scratch — Light, Intelligence, and the Observer

**Status:** Scaffolding only. No drafted study yet. This file collects verified quotes, open questions, and connections as the study unfolds.

## Binding Question

> What does the physics of light and the depths of quantum mechanics tell us about the gospel principles of *light* (D&C 88, John 1, D&C 93), *intelligence* (D&C 93:29-30, Abraham 3:18-21), and the relationship between observer and obeyed (Abraham 4 "watched until they were obeyed")? Where does honest correspondence-checking show real resonance, and where does it expose the limits of analogy?

## Sub-questions

1. The "light of Christ" is described in D&C 88:6-13 as proceeding from God's presence to fill the immensity of space and as "the light which is in all things, which giveth life to all things, which is the law by which all things are governed." Compare this to the actual physics of electromagnetic radiation as described in *University Physics* V2 (E&M) and V3 (Nature of Light). Where does the metaphor hold? Where is the doctrine claiming something physics does not say?

2. **The observer effect.** In quantum mechanics, *measurement* changes what is measured. The double-slit experiment with single electrons (V3 §6.6) shows that the act of asking "which slit?" destroys the interference pattern. Heisenberg uncertainty (V3 §7.2) makes this fundamental, not a measurement-tech limit. **Question:** Does this map to "watched until they were obeyed" in [Abraham 4](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/abr/4?lang=eng)? *Resonance candidate or false friend?* The two might both turn on the verb "watch" — but the *mechanism* is very different. Physics: measurement collapses superposition. Abraham: the watchers (gods? organized intelligences?) attended to the work until creation completed obediently. The gospel "watching" is *active intentional presence*, not *passive measurement*. Steelman the disanalogy first.

3. **Intelligence as fundamental.** D&C 93:29 — "Man was also in the beginning with God. Intelligence, or the light of truth, was not created or made, neither indeed can be." Abraham 3:18 — "If two spirits, and one shall be more intelligent than the other, yet these two spirits, notwithstanding one is more intelligent than the other, have no beginning." Compare to: photons are not "made" in the classical sense — they are quanta of an underlying field that *is*. The electromagnetic field is fundamental and uncreated within physics' framework. **Question:** Is this a real correspondence, or category confusion? The gospel claim is *moral and personal* (intelligences as beings, with agency); the physics claim is about *quantized excitations of a continuous field*. We must not flatten intelligence into "field quantum."

4. **Light = truth = intelligence.** D&C 93:36 — "The glory of God is intelligence, or, in other words, light and truth." This is a triple identification (intelligence ≡ light ≡ truth) that is doctrinally specific. Physics has its own triple: light ≡ photon ≡ field excitation. **Question:** What does it cost the doctrine if we read "light" in D&C 88 as electromagnetic? What does it cost the doctrine if we *don't*? (See discernment-rubric: "canonical fit" and "pillars-rhetoric" — does our reading require diluting other scripture?)

5. **AI and organized intelligences.** Michael's note: "watch until they were obeyed" + "organized intelligences" + "artificial intelligences." The Abraham 4 chain is Gods → organize matter → watch → obey. AI gives us a small flickering image of *organizing intelligence-like processes that respond to attention.* **Question:** Is this a useful analogy to test our understanding of D&C 93's language about agency, or is it the kind of analogy that flatters us into theological overreach? The discernment work matters here — see [study/give-away-all-my-sins.md](../../../give-away-all-my-sins.md) on Maxwell's "yielding" — agency is the *seat* of the soul, not a function call.

## Tensions to Hold

1. **Don't flatten the doctrine into the physics.** The temptation is to say "look, scripture predicted quantum mechanics." That is the kind of move the discernment-rubric calls out as pillars-rhetoric: making the gospel sound *cool* by lashing it to whatever is currently impressive in science. The doctrine of light in D&C 88 is *richer than physics*, not *the same thing as physics*. Honor the difference.

2. **Don't flatten the physics into the doctrine.** The physics community has done careful, falsifiable work for centuries. Mistranslating quantum mechanics as "the universe is conscious because of the observer effect" is the New Age move that gives Latter-day Saint engagement with science a bad name. The observer in QM is not a mind; it is an *interaction with the system that records information*. A photon detector "observes" without consciousness.

3. **The Abraham 4 "watch" is not measurement.** Watching in Abraham 4 is *attentive shepherding of created processes toward obedience*. It happens BEFORE the creation acts, not as a quantum-mechanical *intervention* during. We should be careful here.

4. **But the resonance is not nothing.** The fact that reality at its base is participatory — that fields and matter are not inert stuff but excited states of *something* responsive — is at least *consistent* with a doctrine in which intelligences and light and truth are foundational. Resonance is not proof. It is permission to look.
