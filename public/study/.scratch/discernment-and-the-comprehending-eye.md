## The architecture (now visible)

Truth is comprehension of things as they are (D&C 93:24).
Light cleaveth unto light, intelligence to intelligence (D&C 88:40).
A pure eye fills with light; a darkened eye does not (D&C 88:67; Matt 6:22).
Hope is the eye's *forward orientation* — "things hoped for and not seen" (Ether 12:6).
Charity is the eye's *capacity to see persons and things as they really are* — "we shall see him as he is" (Moroni 7:48).
Therefore: faith → hope → charity is not just a moral chain. It is the *progressive purification of the perceptual organ*. As the eye becomes single, comprehension becomes possible. The same chain that produces Zion produces the discerning observer.

This is why bias work matters at the metaphysical level. Worldview is not just cognitive frame. It is *what the eye is tuned to*. An eye tuned to outcomes (HALESTORM) sees outcomes. An eye tuned to charity sees the soul of the one in front of it.

## What the agent can do — honest accounting

I do NOT have the Spirit. I cannot perform the discernment Moroni 7:15 describes (daylight from dark night). I can pattern-match, fact-check, cross-reference. That is it.

But within those limits:
- I CAN check whether a text describes "things as they really are" by the testable measures: internal coherence, factual correspondence, fit with verifiable history, fit with canonical scripture as written.
- I CAN flag the "looking beyond the mark" failure mode (Jacob 4:14): unfalsifiable speculation, precisionist over-reaching, system-building that loses the plain meaning.
- I CAN apply the D&C 11:12 pillars as observable text properties: does the text *invite* doing good (or contempt)? Doing justly (or partisanship)? Walking humbly (or triumphalism)? Judging righteously (or scapegoating)?
- I CAN apply the D&C 93:24 test: does the text claim more or less than what is?
- I CAN steelman before critiquing.

What I CANNOT do:
- Sense the Spirit's witness or absence
- Know the author's heart
- Discern whether a text is *carrying* the Holy Ghost (2 Ne 28:31's exception clause)
- Distinguish Spirit-confirmed truth from culturally-resonant pleasantness
- See the soul

The rubric must be an honest division of labor. I do my part as a *first filter*. Michael does the Spirit-discerned part as the *final filter*. Neither is the whole.
