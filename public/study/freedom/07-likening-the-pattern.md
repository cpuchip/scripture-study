## The honest counterweight (surface_tensions — what would make this false / keep us sober)
- **The recurring failure of date-mapping.** Every generation that mapped its wars and plagues onto the prophecies and felt "it's now" has so far been wrong about the timing (right about the *watchfulness*). That doesn't falsify the pattern — it disciplines our confidence. We can say "the pattern is real and we are in it" without "and therefore the end is in N years."
- **The conditional escape (Ether 2).** None of this is fixed. "Not until the fulness of iniquity" + "that ye may repent." A nation — or a person — can stay the sweeping-off by turning. The mappings must never harden into fatalism; the whole point is that the door is open.
- **The mirror, not the spyglass.** The danger of mapping is that it turns the eye *outward* (decode the nation, the dates, the signs) when the texts turn it *inward* (am I in the taught category? am I marked with the tav — do I grieve the abominations, or build them?). Keep the table a mirror.
- **Which remnant (provenance/02 §weak #3).** If we liken ourselves to the Nephite *nation*, the type ends at Cumorah; if to the *remnant who met Christ*, it ends at Zion. The mappings only help if they drive the choice to be the remnant — for Michael and his house.
