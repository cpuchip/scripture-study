# Freedom study — carry-forward threads

Threads to pull when there's budget — analyzing the founding documents (now local in
[`books/USA/`](../../books/USA/README.md)) against [*Without Compulsion*](99-synthesis.md).

1. **"endowed by their Creator with certain unalienable Rights"** (Declaration) — agency as
   *God-given, not state-granted*; the actual text behind Dunford's Class 3 hinge. Trace
   against the study's "freedom God will not compel."
2. **Article V** (Constitution) — the amendment process as the structural embodiment of
   *by persuasion, not compulsion*: even the supreme law changes only by consent, never
   seizure. The anti-Cain mechanism in constitutional form.
3. **"We the People"** vs. the two freedoms — a covenant *freely entered*; the political form
   of Abel's freely-given offering. Consent as the ground of legitimate power.
4. **D&C 98:5** — "that law of the land which is constitutional… belongs to all mankind" read
   beside the actual constitutional text it points to ([`books/USA/constitution.md`](../../books/USA/constitution.md));
   test Dunford's Declaration-tie against both.

Source texts (verbatim NARA): `books/USA/` — Declaration, Constitution, Bill of Rights,
Amendments XI–XXVII. Companion: [yt-dunford-constitution-restoration.md](yt-dunford-constitution-restoration.md).
