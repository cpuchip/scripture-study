## 1. Eclipse facts [ESTABLISHED]
- **2017 Aug 21 "Great American Eclipse"** — coast-to-coast US (OR→SC, 14 states incl. Missouri). Max totality 2m41s at Giant City State Park, just south of **Carbondale, IL**. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_August_21,_2017)
- **2024 Apr 8 "Great North American Eclipse"** — Mexico→US→Canada (TX→ME); through Poplar Bluff MO, Paducah KY, **Carbondale IL**, Cleveland, Buffalo. [NASA](https://science.nasa.gov/eclipses/future-eclipses/eclipse-2024/where-when/)
- **The "X" (2017 × 2024 centerlines): Makanda, just south of Carbondale, southern ILLINOIS** — not Missouri proper. Full-width overlap zone (~9,000 sq mi) touches **MO + IL + KY**. ["Eclipse Crossroads"](https://time.com/4904765/solar-eclipse-crossroads-carbondale-illinois/)
  - **CORRECTION 1:** Michael recalled "X over Missouri." Centerline crossing = Carbondale, S. Illinois; the overlap *zone* fairly includes MO/IL/KY.
  - **HONESTY:** The **"New Madrid seismic zone" link is an interpretive overlay** — NASA/astronomers do NOT connect the eclipses to New Madrid. Geographic vicinity only; the *connection* is commentary, not fact.
- **2027 Aug 2 "Eclipse of the Century"** — Spain/Gibraltar → Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, **Egypt**, Sudan, Somalia → **Saudi Arabia, Yemen**. Max totality 6m22s SE of Luxor (longest over land this century). **Michael was RIGHT: this is the Middle East.** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_August_2,_2027) · [NSO map](https://nso.edu/eclipse-map-2027-aug2/)
- **2033 Mar 30 "Great Bering Strait Eclipse"** — Bering Sea → **Chukotka (E. Russia) → northern ALASKA** (Nome, Seward Peninsula, St. Lawrence Island, Kotzebue, Utqiaġvik). An **Arctic** event; no part touches the Middle East. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_March_30,_2033) · [KNOM](https://knom.org/2017/08/23/coming-in-2033-the-great-bering-strait-eclipse/)
  - **UPDATE (Michael clarified 2026-05-31): he meant March 20, 2034, NOT 2033.** The 2033 Alaska eclipse is a red herring; ignore it.
- **2034 Mar 20 total solar eclipse [ESTABLISHED — verified this session via Wikipedia].** Path: Benin → Nigeria → Cameroon → Chad → Sudan → **Egypt → Saudi Arabia → Kuwait → Iran → Afghanistan → Pakistan → India → China.** Greatest eclipse 10:18:45 UTC, totality 4m9s. **Its path crosses the Aug 2, 2027 path "at a location on the southeastern coast of Egypt"** → a SECOND "X," over the Middle East, paralleling the 2017×2024 X over America. Source: [en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_March_20,_2034](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_March_20,_2034).
  - **The corrected geometry HOLDS:** two cross-shaped crossings — America (Carbondale IL, 2017×2024) and Egypt (SE coast, 2027×2034); each pair ~6.6 yrs apart (≈7); ~3.3 yrs (≈3.5, "time, times, and half a time," Dan 7:25 / Rev 12:14) separate the 2024 and 2027 eclipses.
  - **HONEST CAVEAT (whatever the truth brings):** any two non-parallel eclipse paths cross *somewhere* on the globe — a crossing is geometrically common, not in itself miraculous. What is notable is the *locations* (America, then Egypt / the biblical Near East) and the cross/X shape. The crossing is [ESTABLISHED] fact; its *meaning* is [INTERPRETIVE/SPECULATIVE].

## 2. Scriptural signs-language [ESTABLISHED] (verbatim; Read the files before quoting in the study)
- **Luke 21:25** — "signs in the sun, and in the moon, and in the stars; and upon the earth distress of nations..."
- **Joel 2:30–31** — "wonders in the heavens... The sun shall be turned into darkness, and the moon into blood, before the great and the terrible day of the Lord come."
- **Matthew 24:29** — "the sun be darkened, and the moon shall not give her light, and the stars shall fall from heaven..."
- **D&C 45:39–42** — "he that feareth me shall be **looking forth**... for the signs of the coming of the Son of Man... blood, and fire, and vapors of smoke... the sun shall be darkened, and the moon be turned into blood, and the stars fall."
- **D&C 88:87** — "the sun shall hide his face... the moon shall be bathed in blood; and the stars shall become exceedingly angry..."
- **HONEST EXEGETICAL CAUTION:** every passage pairs **sun darkened + moon to blood** (+ falling stars, shaken earth). A *solar eclipse* darkens the sun but does NOT turn the moon to blood (a "blood moon" is a separate *lunar* eclipse). So the prophesied imagery is **not a literal one-to-one match for a solar eclipse alone.** D&C 45:39 is the one verse that legitimately commends *watching* — warrant for faithful watchfulness, not for decoding a specific eclipse.

## Clear-eyed summary (the frame for topic 06)
- **FACT:** 4 real eclipses on those dates; 2017×2024 X at Carbondale (overlap MO/IL/KY); 2027 = Middle East (Michael right); **2033 = Arctic/Alaska (Michael corrected)**; New Madrid link = overlay.
- **FAITHFUL WATCHING (warranted):** "looking forth... for the signs" (D&C 45:39); the celestial signs are real prophecy; prophets urge readiness; feeling the nearness and being moved to prepare is squarely inside the faith.
- **SPECULATION (label clearly, do NOT endorse):** that these particular eclipses are *the* prophesied signs; that the X "marks" anything; that the dates encode a timeline; that the geography converges on the Holy Land (it doesn't — 2033 breaks it). The Church cautioned against exactly this in 2015.
- **The honest landing:** eclipses can rightly stir wonder and turn the heart to watchfulness — but treating them as decoded prophetic signposts crosses from faithful watching into sign-seeking, which scripture and the Brethren caution against. **Use the eclipses as wonder-that-turns-us-to-readiness, not as a cipher.**
