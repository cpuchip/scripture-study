# Provenance — The Destruction, the Two Houses, and the Countdown

*Source: background research subagent G, 2026-05-31. All scripture verbatim via gospel_get this session. Labels per 00-frame.*

## 1. 3 Nephi 8-9 — the destruction + the cities named [ESTABLISHED]
- **3 Ne 8** (AD 34): storm/tempest/"terrible thunder... shake the whole earth" (8:5-7); Zarahemla takes fire (8:8), Moroni sinks in the sea (8:9), Moronihah covered, "a great mountain" (8:10); rocks "rent in twain... in seams and in cracks" (8:18); quaking ~"three hours" (8:19); then "thick darkness," a felt "vapor of darkness," no fire kindled, "**three days** that there was no light seen" (8:20-23). The survivors already cry "O that we had repented... and had not killed and stoned the prophets" (8:24-25).
- **3 Ne 9** — Christ's voice "among all the inhabitants of the earth": "Wo... it is because of their iniquity and abominations that they are fallen" (9:2). Cities + reasons: Zarahemla **burned** (9:3); Moroni **sunk/drowned** (9:4); Moronihah **covered** "to hide their iniquities... that the blood of the prophets and the saints shall not come any more unto me against them" (9:5); Gilgal sunk (9:6); Onihah, Mocum, Jerusalem — waters (9:7); Gadiandi, Gadiomnah, Jacob, Gimgimno — hills/valleys (9:8); **Jacobugath burned "because of their secret murders and combinations; for it was they that did destroy the peace of my people and the government of the land"** (9:9); Laman, Josh, Gad, Kishkumen — burned for "casting out the prophets, and stoning those whom I did send" (9:10-11). Refrain (5×): destroyed "to hide their wickedness... that the blood of the prophets and the saints shall not come up... against them."
- **THE MERCY INSIDE THE JUDGMENT — 3 Ne 9:13-14 [ESTABLISHED]:** "O all ye that are spared because ye were **more righteous** than they, will ye not now return unto me, and repent of your sins, and be converted, **that I may heal you**?... mine arm of mercy is extended towards you, and whosoever will come, him will I receive." The two houses in one verse: those who fell, and "all ye that are spared because ye were more righteous." Then Christ names Himself (9:15-18) + "a broken heart and a contrite spirit" (9:20).

## 2. The two-houses / sorting doctrine [ESTABLISHED text; INTERPRETIVE-faithful synthesis]
The same event sorts; it does not CREATE the division — it reveals what was already chosen.
- **Wheat & tares — D&C 86:7** "let the wheat and the tares grow together until the harvest... then ye shall first gather out the wheat... the tares are bound in bundles... to be burned" (86:6: "pluck not... lest you destroy the wheat"). **Matt 13:30, 38-43** — "the field is the world... the good seed are the children of the kingdom; the tares are the children of the wicked one"; "the righteous shine forth as the sun."
- **Sheep & goats — Matt 25:32-33, 46** — "separate them one from another"; "everlasting punishment: **but** the righteous into life eternal."
- **★ Malachi 4:1-2 [ESTABLISHED] — the sharpest single witness (ONE day, two outcomes):** "the day cometh, that shall burn as an oven; and all the proud... shall be stubble... **But** unto you that fear my name shall the Sun of righteousness arise with **healing** in his wings." The same day burns the proud and heals the faithful. **Word-link [INTERPRETIVE-faithful]:** "healing" appears in BOTH Mal 4:2 AND 3 Ne 9:13 — the fire that destroys the wicked is the healing of the righteous.
- **D&C 88:94-98** — Second Coming as the same sort (tares "bound in bundles... ready to be burned"; the saints "caught up to meet him").
- **Synthesis [INTERPRETIVE-faithful]:** the destructive day reveals/enacts a division the people's prior choices already made. 3 Ne 8-9 is the BoM's enacted instance — the buried cities and "all ye that are spared because ye were more righteous" = tares and wheat of one harvest. → grounds Michael's "we choose, and the choice sorts us into two houses."

## 4. The countdown pattern + the FIVE-years correction [ESTABLISHED]
- **CORRECTION CONFIRMED: Helaman 14:2 reads "five years more cometh," NOT six.** "I give unto you a sign; for **five years** more cometh, and behold, then cometh the Son of God." 14:3-4: the sign defined (a night with no darkness, "one day and a night and a day, as if it were one day"). 14:20-27: the death-signs (3 days darkness, rocks rent "in seams and in cracks," "many highways shall be broken up, and many cities shall become desolate").
- **Fulfilled exactly — 3 Ne 1:15-21:** "at the going down of the sun there was no darkness" (1:15); "no darkness in all that night" (1:19); "all things, every whit, according to the words of the prophets" (1:20); "a new star did appear" (1:21).
- **Pattern [INTERPRETIVE-faithful]:** prophetic warning → defined interval ("five years") → sign fulfilled to the day. Hel 14 → 3 Ne 1 is the clean instance. **BUT (discipline):** the five years was a *specifically revealed* interval for the birth sign — no comparable interval has been revealed for us. Applying a fixed countdown to our day = date-setting (07 §4: hold back).
