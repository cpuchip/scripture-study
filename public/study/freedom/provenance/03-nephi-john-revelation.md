# Provenance — Did Nephi See Us, and Does John Continue the Vision?

*Source: background research subagent E, 2026-05-31. Scripture verbatim via gospel_get; manual/curriculum labeled as curriculum (not canon). Labels per 00-frame. This agent surfaced the firmest anchor of the whole study AND two clean "not-doctrine" corrections.*

## 1. 1 Nephi 13 — Nephi saw the latter-day Gentiles on this land [ESTABLISHED text; specific IDs INTERPRETIVE]
- The man "wrought upon" crossing "many waters" to the promised land (13:12, traditionally Columbus — **text names no one**).
- Gentiles "went forth out of captivity, upon the many waters... did prosper and obtain the land for their inheritance... did humble themselves before the Lord; and the power of the Lord was with them" (13:13-16, traditionally the colonists/pilgrims).
- "Their **mother Gentiles** were gathered together... to battle against them... they were **delivered by the power of God** out of the hands of all other nations" (13:17-19, traditionally the Revolution).
- **CAVEAT:** Columbus / pilgrims / Revolution as the specific referents = **[INTERPRETIVE — faithful, traditional]**; the text says "a man," "other Gentiles," "mother Gentiles," names no persons or wars. The *arc* (Spirit-led crossing → deliverance from captivity → a free people prospering on a covenant land) is on the page — and it's the freedom thread in seed form.
- Plain & precious things taken away (13:26-29, "Satan hath great power over them") + restored in the last days (13:34-42, the BoM + Bible "established in one") = [ESTABLISHED].

## 2. ★ 1 Nephi 14:18-27 — the WARRANT that John's Revelation continues Nephi's vision [ESTABLISHED, canonical — the firmest anchor]
"one of the twelve apostles of the Lamb" (14:20); "**he shall see and write the remainder of these things**... and also many things which have been" (14:21); "he shall also write concerning **the end of the world**" (14:22); Nephi forbidden to write the rest, "for the Lord God hath ordained the apostle of the Lamb of God that he should write them" (14:25); "the name of the apostle of the Lamb was **John**" (14:27); "I, Nephi, am forbidden that I should write the remainder" (14:28). Church footnotes on vv.20,21,27 → Revelation 1:1. **So "John's Revelation continues Nephi's forbidden vision" is canonical, not overlay. Build on this.**

## 3. Revelation structure [ESTABLISHED headings/text; "13-16 = today" NOT in the canon's key]
- **Rev 12** — woman/man-child/dragon/wilderness/war; "the dragon... went to make war with the remnant of her seed, which keep the commandments of God, and have the testimony of Jesus Christ" (12:17).
- **Rev 13** — two beasts; the beast makes "war with the saints" with power "over all kindreds" (13:7); the **mark "in their right hand, or in their foreheads," "no man might buy or sell, save he that had the mark"** (13:16-17).
- **Rev 7** — the sealing: "Hurt not the earth... till we have **sealed the servants of our God in their foreheads**" (7:2-3); the 144,000 (7:4). **D&C 77:8-11 (the Lord's own key)** confirms Rev 7 = the sixth seal, the angel from the east = "Elias... to gather... the tribes of Israel and restore all things," the 144,000 = high priests "to administer the everlasting gospel." → Rev 7 latter-day sealing = [ESTABLISHED].
- **★ THE BEAST-MARK vs GOD-SEAL SYMMETRY [INTERPRETIVE-faithful, strong]:** in Rev 13 the **beast** marks people (forehead/hand, to buy/sell); in Rev 7 **God** seals His servants in the forehead. A deliberate counter-seal — **which IS the tav thread (provenance/06 §4):** two marks, two houses; the tav of Christ on the remnant vs. the mark of the beast. Powerful, and scripturally anchored (both marks are forehead-marks in the text).
- **"Rev 13-16 = our specific day" = [INTERPRETIVE/SPECULATIVE — flag plainly]:** NOT stated in Revelation. **Decisively: D&C 77 (the Lord's verse-by-verse key) interprets Rev 1-11 and STOPS — it never assigns chapters 12-16 to a present-day window.** The canonical thousand-year framework attaches to the seven **seals** (D&C 77:6-7), not to chapter numbers. The canon had the chance to pin 13-16 to a calendar and didn't. Treat any tight mapping as one reader's interpretation.

## 4. Seven churches = seven dispensations? → [NOT DOCTRINE — CORRECTION for 07 §5]
- **D&C 77:5 (the Lord's key)** treats the seven churches as **literal first-century branches**: the twenty-four elders "belonged to the seven churches, and were then in the paradise of God" — dead first-century elders, incompatible with the churches being seven future ages.
- **2024 Church curriculum (Revelation 1-5):** the seven churches = "seven branches of the Church in Asia Minor"; "seven may have simply been symbolic, representing the entire Christian Church."
- gospel_search returned **no** authoritative source teaching "seven churches = seven dispensations Adam→Joseph Smith." → It is **independent commentary / dispensationalist tradition, NOT scripture, NOT the Church's reading.** Flag unambiguously or omit. The textual default: seven real branches, "seven" = completeness/the whole Church.

## 5. Adam-Ondi-Ahman — [ESTABLISHED canon + prophetic elaboration]
- **D&C 116:** "Spring Hill is named by the Lord Adam-ondi-Ahman, because... it is the place where **Adam shall come to visit his people, or the Ancient of Days shall sit,** as spoken of by Daniel."
- **D&C 107:53-57:** the past gathering — "three years previous to the death of Adam," he gathered his righteous posterity "into the valley of Adam-ondi-Ahman... bestowed his last blessing"; "the Lord appeared... called him Michael, the prince, the archangel."
- **Daniel 7:9-14:** the Ancient of Days enthroned; "one like the Son of man came... and there was given him dominion... an everlasting dominion." **D&C 27:11:** Ancient of Days = Adam/Michael.
- **The future council** (all dispensations gathered, keys delivered up to Christ before His coming) = **prophetic teaching** (Joseph Smith; Joseph Fielding Smith) now in official curriculum (Daniel 1-7 helps: "at Adam-ondi-Ahman... will involve Saints from all dispensations"). No date-setting in any source.
