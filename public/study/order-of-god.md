## Part 1: The Name Behind the Name

We call it "the Melchizedek Priesthood," but that's a nickname. The real name tells us everything:

> **3.** Before his day it was called *the Holy Priesthood, after the Order of the Son of God.*
>
> **4.** But out of respect or reverence to the name of the Supreme Being, to avoid the too frequent repetition of his name, they, the church, in ancient days, called that priesthood after Melchizedek, or the Melchizedek Priesthood.
> — [D&C 107:3-4](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p3-p4#p3)

So the proper name is: **The Holy Priesthood, after the Order of the Son of God.**

Not just "the priesthood." Not just "holy priesthood." It is a priesthood *after an order* — and that order is the Son of God's own order. The word "order" here isn't incidental. It carries weight.

### Webster 1828 on "Order"

The 1828 dictionary offers a cascade of relevant meanings:

1. **"Regular arrangement; any methodical or established succession or harmonious relation; method; system."** — The holy order is a *system*, a pattern of how things are arranged.
2. **"Conformity with law or decorum; freedom from disturbance; general tranquillity."** — Order as the opposite of chaos. Order as *rest*.
3. **"A body of persons having some common honorary distinction or rule of obligation."** — An order of people, called and set apart.
4. **"An ecclesiastical grade or rank... the office of the Christian ministry... to take holy orders."** — The priestly meaning directly.

All four meanings are active here simultaneously. The holy order of God is:
- A **system** — the eternal pattern by which God operates
- A **state of harmony** — conformity with law, freedom from disturbance
- A **body of people** — those called and ordained to this order
- A **sacred office** — the priesthood itself

### Webster 1828 on "Ordain"

> 1. "To set in order; to arrange according to rule; to regulate; to set; to establish."
> 2. "To set apart for an office; to appoint."
> 3. "To invest with ministerial or sacerdotal functions."

To be *ordained* is to be *set in order* — placed into an existing arrangement. You don't create the order when you're ordained. You enter it. The order existed before you and will exist after you. It is "without beginning of days or end of years" ([Alma 13:7](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p7#p7)).

## Part 2: Alma 13 — The Definitive Text

Alma 13 is the most sustained treatment of the holy order in all of scripture. Alma is preaching to the people of Ammonihah, and he takes them deep into the nature of priesthood. Let's walk through it carefully.

### The Calling (vv. 1-5)

> **1.** ...the Lord God ordained priests, after his holy order, which was after the order of his Son, to teach these things unto the people.
>
> **2.** And those priests were ordained after the order of his Son, in a manner that thereby the people might know in what manner to look forward to his Son for redemption.
> — [Alma 13:1-2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p1-p2#p1)

The purpose of the order is redemption-pointing. The priesthood isn't about *administration* first — it's about pointing people toward the Son. The manner of ordination itself teaches "in what manner to look forward to his Son." The priesthood is a type of Christ.

> **3.** And this is the manner after which they were ordained—being **called and prepared from the foundation of the world** according to the foreknowledge of God, on account of their exceeding faith and good works; in the first place being left to **choose** good or evil; therefore they having chosen good, and exercising exceedingly great faith, are called with a holy calling, yea, with that holy calling which was prepared with, and according to, a preparatory redemption for such.
> — [Alma 13:3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p3#p3)

This is staggering. The calling to the holy order:
- Was **prepared from the foundation of the world** — it predates mortality
- Depends on **foreknowledge** — God knew who would choose
- Requires **agency** — "in the first place being left to choose"
- Follows from **faith and good works** — not arbitrary selection
- Is connected to "a **preparatory redemption**" — the order and the Atonement are linked from the beginning

> **4-5.** And thus they have been called to this holy calling on account of their faith, while others would reject the Spirit of God... if it had not been for this they might have had as great privilege as their brethren. Or in fine, **in the first place they were on the same standing with their brethren**...
> — [Alma 13:4-5](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p4-p5#p4)

Equal starting position. The difference is *choice*. The holy order is available to all — it is those who "would not harden their hearts" who are called to it. The order is not a caste system; it is a covenant response to agency exercised in faith.

### The Nature of the Order (vv. 6-9)

> **6.** And thus being called by this holy calling, and ordained unto the high priesthood of the holy order of God, to teach his commandments unto the children of men, **that they also might enter into his rest**—
> — [Alma 13:6](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p6#p6)

The destination of the order is **rest**. We'll come back to this — it's the key.

> **7.** This high priesthood being after the order of his Son, which order was from the foundation of the world; or in other words, being **without beginning of days or end of years, being prepared from eternity to all eternity**, according to his foreknowledge of all things—
> — [Alma 13:7](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p7#p7)

The order is **eternal**. Not created at some point — it was "prepared from eternity to all eternity." Without beginning. Without end.

> **8.** ...being called with a holy calling, and ordained with a holy ordinance, and taking upon them the high priesthood of the holy order, **which calling, and ordinance, and high priesthood, is without beginning or end**—
> — [Alma 13:8](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p8#p8)

The calling is without beginning or end. The ordinance is without beginning or end. The priesthood is without beginning or end. This isn't just "old" — this is *eternal in both directions.*

> **9.** Thus they become high priests forever, after the order of the Son, the Only Begotten of the Father, who is **without beginning of days or end of years, who is full of grace, equity, and truth**. And thus it is. Amen.
> — [Alma 13:9](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p9#p9)

The Son himself is "full of grace, equity, and truth." The order of the priesthood carries the character of the one whose order it is. Grace. Equity. Truth.

### Sanctification and Rest (vv. 10-13)

> **10-11.** ...there were many who were ordained and became high priests of God; and it was on account of their exceeding faith and repentance, and their righteousness before God... Therefore they were called after this holy order, and were **sanctified**, and their **garments were washed white through the blood of the Lamb**.
> — [Alma 13:10-11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p10-p11#p10)

> **12.** Now they, after being **sanctified by the Holy Ghost**, having their garments made white, being **pure and spotless before God**, could not look upon sin save it were with abhorrence; and there were many, exceedingly great many, who were made pure and **entered into the rest of the Lord their God**.
> — [Alma 13:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p12#p12)

The progression:
1. Called → on account of faith and good works
2. Ordained → after the holy order
3. Sanctified → by the Holy Ghost, washed white through the blood of the Lamb
4. Purified → could not look upon sin
5. **Entered into the rest** of the Lord their God

The order is a *pathway*, not just a title. It moves through sanctification toward rest.

### The Melchizedek Example (vv. 14-19)

> **17-18.** Now this Melchizedek was a king over the land of Salem; and his people had waxed strong in iniquity and abomination... But Melchizedek having exercised mighty faith, and received the office of the high priesthood according to the holy order of God, **did preach repentance unto his people. And behold, they did repent; and Melchizedek did establish peace in the land** in his days; therefore he was called the **prince of peace**, for he was the king of Salem; and he did reign under his father.
> — [Alma 13:17-18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p17-p18#p17)

Melchizedek is the exemplar because he did what the order is *for*: he brought an entire people from iniquity to peace. He is the "prince of peace" — *šālôm*, wholeness, completeness, rest. His name tells the story: *Malkî-ṣedeq*, "my king is righteousness." He is a living type of the Son of God, whose order this is.

> **19.** Now, there were many before him, and also there were many afterwards, but **none were greater**; therefore, of him they have more particularly made mention.
> — [Alma 13:19](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p19#p19)

None were greater. That's why the priesthood bears his name — not because the order began with him, but because he exemplified it most perfectly among mortals.

## Part 3: The Priesthood Lineage — A Chain Without Beginning

D&C 84 traces the priesthood lineage backward from Moses all the way to Adam:

> Moses ← Jethro ← Caleb ← Elihu ← Jeremy ← Gad ← Esaias ← ...Abraham ← **Melchizedek** ← ...through the lineage of his fathers... ← Noah ← Enoch ← Abel ← **Adam**
> — [D&C 84:6-16](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p6-p16#p6)

And then the punchline:

> **17.** Which priesthood continueth in the church of God in all generations, and is **without beginning of days or end of years**.
> — [D&C 84:17](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p17#p17)

The lineage traces *back* to Adam, but the priesthood itself is "without beginning." It doesn't originate with Adam — Adam received it. And how did Adam enter this order?

> **67.** And thou art after the **order of him who was without beginning of days or end of years**, from all eternity to all eternity.
>
> **68.** Behold, thou art **one in me, a son of God**; and thus may all become my sons.
> — [Moses 6:67-68](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/6?lang=eng&id=p67-p68#p67)

Adam was told: "thou art after the order of him who was without beginning of days or end of years." He entered the eternal order. And the promise: "thus may **all** become my sons." The order is open.

### Abraham Sought It

> **2.** ...I sought for the blessings of the fathers, and the right whereunto I should be ordained to administer the same; having been myself a follower of righteousness, desiring also to be **one who possessed great knowledge**, and to be a greater follower of righteousness, and to **possess a greater knowledge**...
>
> **3.** It was conferred upon me from the fathers; it came down from the fathers, **from the beginning of time, yea, even from the beginning, or before the foundation of the earth**, down to the present time...
> — [Abraham 1:2-3](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/abr/1?lang=eng&id=p2-p3#p2)

Abraham *desired* the blessings of the fathers. He *sought* the priesthood. He wanted to "possess great knowledge." And the priesthood came down "from before the foundation of the earth." The same language as Alma 13:3 — "prepared from the foundation of the world."

D&C 84 tells us plainly:

> **19.** And this greater priesthood administereth the gospel and holdeth **the key of the mysteries of the kingdom, even the key of the knowledge of God**.
>
> **20.** Therefore, in the ordinances thereof, **the power of godliness is manifest**.
>
> **21.** And without the ordinances thereof, and the authority of the priesthood, the power of godliness **is not manifest** unto men in the flesh;

The purpose of the order is to make the *power of godliness* manifest — to bring people into the knowledge and presence of God. Without it, that power is hidden. The order is the mechanism by which intelligence (the glory of God) is transmitted, received, and comprehended.

## Part 5: The Connection — Order, Truth, Intelligence, and One Eternal Round

Here's where your intuition about the connection to our truth and intelligence studies lands.

### The Order IS the Pattern of Truth

In our [truth study](./truth.md), we identified what we called "The Circle of Existence":

```
        INTELLIGENCE (uncreated, self-acting)
              ↓ clothed in
        SPIRIT (fine matter)
              ↓ clothed in
        ELEMENT (physical matter)
              ↓ governed by
           LAW
              ↓ enabling
         AGENCY → OPPOSITION → CHOICE
              ↓ through obedience
      TRUTH AND LIGHT RECEIVED
              ↓ grace for grace
     GLORIFICATION → FULNESS → COMPREHENSION
              ↓ which is
        INTELLIGENCE (now at a higher sphere)
```

Look at what Alma 13 describes:

1. **Intelligence acts** — "in the first place being left to choose good or evil" (v.3)
2. **Choice is exercised** — "they having chosen good, and exercising exceedingly great faith" (v.3)
3. **Calling comes** — "called with a holy calling" (v.3)
4. **Ordination into the order** — "ordained unto the high priesthood of the holy order" (v.6)
5. **Sanctification through obedience** — "sanctified... garments washed white" (v.11)
6. **Purification** — "pure and spotless before God" (v.12)
7. **Entry into rest** — "entered into the rest of the Lord their God" (v.12)

This IS the circle of existence applied to the priesthood. The order of God *is* the operating pattern of how intelligence progresses from agency through sanctification to fulness (rest). The priesthood is the *administered form* of the eternal pattern we studied in truth and intelligence.

### The "Rest" Is the Fulness of Glory

What is "the rest of the Lord"? D&C 84 defines it:

> **24.** ...they should not enter into his rest while in the wilderness, which rest is **the fulness of his glory**.
> — [D&C 84:24](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p24#p24)

The "rest" is the fulness of God's glory. And what is the glory of God?

> **36.** The **glory of God is intelligence**, or, in other words, **light and truth**.
> — [D&C 93:36](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93?lang=eng&id=p36#p36)

So the rest of the Lord = the fulness of his glory = the fulness of intelligence = the fulness of light and truth.

The holy order leads to *the fulness of intelligence*. The priesthood is the covenant pathway to comprehending all things — to becoming "full of grace, equity, and truth" as the Son is ([Alma 13:9](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p9#p9)).

And how did the Son get there?

> **12-13.** ...he received not of the fulness at the first, but received grace for grace; And he received not of the fulness at first, but continued **from grace to grace, until he received a fulness**.
> — [D&C 93:12-13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93?lang=eng&id=p12-p13#p12)

Grace for grace. Line upon line. The same progressive, sanctifying, light-receiving pattern that Alma 13 describes for those who enter the holy order.

Now the "one eternal round" connection:

> **20.** ...he cannot walk in crooked paths; neither doth he vary from that which he hath said; neither hath he a shadow of turning from the right to the left, or from that which is right to that which is wrong; therefore, **his course is one eternal round**.

The word "round" is fascinating. God's course is *straight* (He doesn't vary) AND *round* (it's one eternal cycle). These aren't contradictions — they're describing a helix, or more precisely, a pattern that is perfectly consistent (straight/unchanging) while also being self-completing and self-renewing (round/cyclical).

**The order of God IS the one eternal round.** It is the pattern that never changes — the same process of faith → calling → ordination → sanctification → glorification → rest that has operated "from all eternity to all eternity" ([Moses 6:67](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/6?lang=eng&id=p67#p67)). It was the same for Adam, for Enoch, for Melchizedek, for Abraham, and for every soul who enters the covenant.

> **41.** He **comprehendeth all things**, and all things are before him, and all things are **round about him**; and he is above all things, and in all things, and is through all things, and **is round about all things**; and all things are by him, and of him, even God, forever and ever.
> — [D&C 88:41](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88?lang=eng&id=p41#p41)

"Round about him." "Round about all things." The language of the eternal round — God is the center and circumference. All things orbit around, through, and in him. The order is the orbital pattern.

> **42-44.** ...he hath given a law unto all things, by which they move in their **times and their seasons**; And their **courses are fixed**... and they give light to each other in their times...

Fixed courses. Times and seasons. Giving light to each other. Every kingdom in its own **order** ([D&C 88:60](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88?lang=eng&id=p60#p60) — "Every man in his own order").

The cosmos itself operates after the order of God. The planets give light to each other in their times and seasons, just as priesthood holders teach and minister in their callings. The physical universe is a type and shadow of the holy order.

## Part 6: Hebrews 7 — Paul's Witness

Paul understood this, perhaps more deeply than we often give him credit for:

> **3.** Without father, without mother, without descent, having neither beginning of days, nor end of life; but made like unto the Son of God; **abideth a priest continually**.

## Part 7: The Oath and Covenant — The Mechanics of Entry

D&C 84 gives us the mechanics of how one enters and progresses in the order:

> **33.** For whoso is faithful unto the obtaining these two priesthoods... and the **magnifying their calling**, are **sanctified** by the Spirit unto the **renewing of their bodies**.
>
> **34.** They become the sons of Moses and of Aaron and **the seed of Abraham**, and the church and kingdom, and **the elect of God**.
>
> **35-38.** And also all they who receive this priesthood **receive me**, saith the Lord; For he that receiveth my servants receiveth me; And he that receiveth me receiveth my Father; And he that receiveth my Father receiveth my Father's kingdom; therefore **all that my Father hath shall be given unto him**.
>
> **39.** And this is according to **the oath and covenant** which belongeth to the priesthood.
> — [D&C 84:33-39](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p33-p39#p33)

The chain of reception:
$$\text{Receive servants} \rightarrow \text{Receive Christ} \rightarrow \text{Receive Father} \rightarrow \text{Receive all that the Father hath}$$

"All that my Father hath." That's the fulness. That's the rest. That's the glory of God — which is intelligence, light and truth. The oath and covenant is the mechanism by which the eternal round delivers you to fulness.

And look at the remarkable parallel with the truth study. D&C 84:45 weaves truth, light, and Spirit into a single chain:

> **45.** For the **word of the Lord is truth**, and whatsoever is **truth is light**, and whatsoever is **light is Spirit**, even the Spirit of Jesus Christ.
> — [D&C 84:45](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p45#p45)

$$\text{Word of the Lord} = \text{Truth} = \text{Light} = \text{Spirit of Jesus Christ}$$

This is the same equivalence chain from D&C 93 that we built in the truth study. Here in D&C 84, it's explicitly placed *inside* the discussion of the priesthood and its covenant. The holy order is the administered form of the eternal truth-light-intelligence framework.

## Part 8: D&C 77:11 — The 144,000 and the Holy Order

One more stunning connection. In D&C 77, Joseph Smith is told about the 144,000 sealed from all the tribes of Israel:

> **11.** ...those who are sealed are **high priests, ordained unto the holy order of God, to administer the everlasting gospel**; for they are they who are ordained out of every nation, kindred, tongue, and people, by the angels to whom is given power over the nations of the earth, to bring as many as will come to **the church of the Firstborn**.
> — [D&C 77:11](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/77?lang=eng&id=p11#p11)

The 144,000 are high priests after the holy order. They administer the everlasting gospel. They bring people to the church of the Firstborn. And the church of the Firstborn connects back to D&C 93:

> **22.** And all those who are begotten through me are partakers of the glory of the same, and are **the church of the Firstborn**.
> — [D&C 93:22](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93?lang=eng&id=p22#p22)

The holy order brings people to the church of the Firstborn — to share in the glory (intelligence, light and truth) of the Son. The circle completes.

## Part 9: Sanctify — The Mechanism of the Order

If the order is the pattern, sanctification is its process. Webster 1828:

> **SANCTIFY:** 1. "To make sacred or holy; to set apart to a holy or religious use; to consecrate." 2. "To make free from sin; to cleanse from moral corruption and pollution; to purify."

Both meanings are operative. To be sanctified is to be both *set apart* and *made pure*. Alma 13:10-12 shows both: those in the order are set apart (called, ordained) AND made pure (garments washed white, sanctified by the Holy Ghost).

The word also echoes D&C 88:34 — "that which is governed by law is also preserved by law and **perfected and sanctified** by the same." Sanctification happens through law. It is not arbitrary or miraculous in the sense of lawless — it is the natural result of operating within the order, receiving light, and being purified by it.

## Synthesis: The Holy Order IS the Mechanics of Existence, Administered

Here is the thesis of this study:

**The holy order of God is not merely a priesthood. It is the eternal pattern — the operating system — by which intelligence progresses from agency through sanctification to fulness. It is the administered, covenantal form of the mechanics of existence we identified in the truth study.**

Consider:

| Truth Study Framework | Holy Order Framework |
|---|---|
| Intelligence is uncreated, self-acting | "In the first place being left to choose" — agency is the starting point |
| Truth is independent in its sphere | Each person operates "on the same standing" (equal starting position) |
| Intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence | Faith attracts calling; light attracts light |
| Progressive reception of light | "Grace for grace" / sanctification journey |
| Law governs, preserves, and sanctifies | The oath and covenant — the law of the priesthood |
| Fulness = comprehension of all things | Rest = fulness of God's glory = intelligence |
| One eternal round — the circle completes | "Without beginning of days or end of years" |

The order is the *covenant structure* wrapped around the eternal truth. Without the order, the truth exists but remains abstract — "the power of godliness is not manifest unto men in the flesh" ([D&C 84:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p21#p21)). The order makes the truth *operational*. It gives form to the eternal pattern so that mortals can *enter* it, *walk* in it, and be *transformed* by it.

And it is "one eternal round" because it is the same pattern everywhere, always:
- Adam received it ([Moses 6:67](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/moses/6?lang=eng&id=p67#p67))
- Enoch walked in it ([D&C 84:15](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p15#p15))
- Melchizedek exemplified it ([Alma 13:18](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/13?lang=eng&id=p18#p18))
- Abraham sought it ([Abraham 1:2](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/abr/1?lang=eng&id=p2#p2))
- Moses tried to give it to Israel — they refused ([D&C 84:23-25](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p23-p25#p23))
- Christ embodied it perfectly ([D&C 93:12-13](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93?lang=eng&id=p12-p13#p12))
- It continues to be offered today ([D&C 84:42](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84?lang=eng&id=p42#p42))

Same order. Same covenant. Same destination. One eternal round.

## Become

1. **The order is not about position — it's about pattern.** The holy order isn't a hierarchy to climb; it's a pattern to embody. Faith → calling → ordination → sanctification → rest. Every priesthood holder walks this same path. The question isn't "what office do I hold?" but "am I being sanctified by this order?"

2. **"Enter into his rest" is the invitation.** Rest isn't idleness — it's the *fulness of God's glory*, which is intelligence, light and truth. It means reaching a state where you comprehend all things, where you are so filled with light that you *are* light. The priesthood is the covenant path to that.

3. **Agency is the beginning of everything.** Alma 13:3 — "in the first place being left to choose." The order doesn't override agency; it *requires* it. God's eternal pattern begins with the choice to seek the light. Without that choice, "there is no existence" ([D&C 93:30](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93?lang=eng&id=p30#p30)).

4. **The order connects you to everyone who has ever walked this path.** From Adam to now — the same order. When you exercise the priesthood, you are participating in the same pattern that Melchizedek used to establish peace in Salem. The same pattern that Enoch used to build Zion. "One eternal round" means you are not alone in this.

5. **Truth, intelligence, and priesthood are not separate subjects.** They are the same subject viewed from different angles. Truth is the substance. Intelligence is the glory. Priesthood is the covenant structure. The order of God is where all three converge.
