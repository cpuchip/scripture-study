### Headlines

**Apple rebuilds Siri as a standalone chatbot app.** At WWDC 2026, Apple shipped iOS 27 developer beta 1 with a rebuilt Siri—internally codenamed Campos—as a standalone app featuring conversation history, a multi-turn dialogue interface, and Dynamic Island integration with a persistent "Search or Ask" prompt. A new Siri Extensions framework also launched, allowing third-party AI providers to plug into Siri and Apple Intelligence surfaces. The overhaul represents the most significant structural change to Siri since its debut, though Apple has not yet published a detailed official recap, and specifics remain aggregated from secondary sources. ([ChatForest](https://chatforest.com/reviews/apple-wwdc-2026-siri-overhaul-ios-27-apple-intelligence-preview/))

**Perplexity previews hybrid local-cloud inference orchestrator.** Perplexity announced a system—built with Intel and compatible with NVIDIA RTX Spark—that automatically routes AI workloads between a local device and cloud agents based on task sensitivity. A compact on-device model handles sensitive data like financial or health records locally, while heavier tasks route to frontier cloud models. The product ships in July; today's news covers the architecture reveal and technical preview. ([Pulse2](https://pulse2.com/perplexity-announces-first-hybrid-local-server-inference-orchestrator-for-personal-computer/))
