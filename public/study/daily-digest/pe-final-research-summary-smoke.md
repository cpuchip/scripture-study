**Headlines**

**Google ships Gemini 3.5 Flash to billions, previews Spark agent and Omni world model.** At I/O 2026, Google rolled out Gemini 3.5 Flash as the new default in the Gemini app and Search. The company also announced Gemini Spark, a 24/7 personal agent entering beta next week, and Gemini Omni, a world model for generating and editing video from multimodal inputs. The concrete ship today is the Flash model; Spark and Omni remain limited rollouts. [Gemini 3.5: frontier intelligence with action](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-5/)

**GitHub Copilot Spaces API hits general availability.** GitHub made its Spaces API generally available, allowing programmatic creation and management of Copilot contexts. Copilot Business and Enterprise tiers also switched to GPT-5.3-Codex as the base model and gained Gemini 3.5 Flash support. [Copilot Spaces API now generally available](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-05-18-copilot-spaces-api-now-generally-available/)

**Dell and Palantir partner on deskside agentic AI workstations.** Dell launched Deskside Agentic AI workstations that run up to 1T-parameter models locally using NVIDIA NemoClaw and OpenShell. The company also announced a joint on-premises AI operating system with Palantir, bringing Foundry and Ontology to Dell AI Factory infrastructure. [Dell Technologies Delivers Production-Ready Agentic AI](https://www.dell.com/en-us/dt/corporate/newsroom/announcements/detailpage.press-releases~usa~2026~05~dell-technologies-delivers-production-ready-agentic-ai-from-deskside-to-data-center.htm)

**Skeptical takes**
Google's I/O keynote applied its usual transformative framing to the announcements; the only component live at scale today is 3.5 Flash, while Spark and Omni are gated betas. Dell's promises of production readiness and rapid break-even are standard vendor claims that rarely match real enterprise procurement timelines. Zenlytic's self-learning descriptor is marketing language for automated schema detection, not autonomous improvement.

**Carry-forward**
Watch whether Google's Spark beta next week opens broadly or stays limited. The Karpathy-Anthropic pairing is worth monitoring for research direction, but it is not a product ship. No obvious candidate for an immediate deep-research run unless enterprise on-prem agentic AI becomes relevant to active workstreams.
