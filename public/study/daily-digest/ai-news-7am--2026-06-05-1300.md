**Headlines**

OpenAI began rolling out a significantly rebuilt memory architecture for ChatGPT, internally called "Dreaming," that synthesizes scattered user context into a more coherent, compute-efficient profile. The result is fresher, more continuous conversations with better relevance across sessions. Plus and Pro users in the US are getting it first; free and Go users will follow over the coming weeks. [OpenAI "Dreaming" Memory Architecture for ChatGPT](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-memory-dreaming/)

NVIDIA released Nemotron 3 Ultra, a 550B-parameter open-weights Mixture-of-Experts model that activates only 55B parameters per forward pass. Built with hybrid Mamba-Transformer layers and NVFP4 quantization, it is aimed at long-running agent workflows. NVIDIA claims roughly five times the throughput and up to thirty percent lower task-completion cost than comparable open alternatives, releasing weights, training data, and recipes together. [NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Ultra](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/nvidia-nemotron-3-ultra-powers-faster-more-efficient-reasoning-for-long-running-agents/)

A University of Cambridge–led team published Phase I results in the *Journal of Infection* for a universal Sarbeco coronavirus vaccine whose active super-antigen was designed entirely through computer simulation. The trial in 39 human volunteers showed the vaccine was safe and elicited immune responses against SARS-CoV-2, SARS, and related bat coronaviruses. [Cambridge / DIOSynVax AI-Designed Vaccine](https://www.cam.ac.uk/research/news/new-universal-vaccine-technology-could-protect-us-from-future-virus-outbreaks)

**Notable**

Microsoft AI launched seven new in-house models alongside "Frontier Tuning" for enterprise customization and a partnership with Mayo Clinic to co-develop a clinical foundation model owned by Mayo. [Microsoft AI New Models](https://microsoft.ai/news/building-a-hillclimbing-machine-launching-seven-new-mai-models/)

H Company released Holo3.1, a family of quantized computer-use agents sized from 0.8B to 35B parameters, with checkpoints in FP8, Q4 GGUF, and NVFP4 meant to run GUI-automation tasks locally on consumer hardware. [Holo3.1 Local & Quantized Agents](https://huggingface.co/blog/hcompany/holo31)

**Carry-forward**

Watch for independent benchmarks verifying Nemotron 3 Ultra's throughput and cost claims against other open agent models. The Cambridge vaccine data merits a deeper look if Phase II is announced. OpenAI's Dreaming memory rollout to free-tier users will signal whether the efficiency gains are substantial enough to drop the paywall.
