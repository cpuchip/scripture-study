# D&C 88 — the olive leaf: the light of Christ, the law of all kingdoms, and the house of learning

**Setting:** **Kirtland, Ohio, December 27-28, 1832, and January 3, 1833.** At a conference, high priests had prayed "separately and vocally to the Lord to reveal his will unto us concerning the upbuilding of Zion." Joseph called the result the "'**olive leaf**'… plucked from the Tree of Paradise, the Lord's message of peace to us." It is one of the grandest revelations in the canon: the second Comforter, the cosmic Light of Christ, the law that governs every kingdom, the resurrection, the call to "seek learning even by study and also by faith," and the founding of the School of the Prophets.

**Binding idea:** "The light which proceedeth forth from the presence of God to fill the immensity of space— the light which is in all things, which giveth life to all things, **which is the law by which all things are governed**, even the power of God" (88:12-13). The Light of Christ is not metaphor: it fills all space, animates all life, and *is* the law by which everything is governed — and what follows is the unfolding of that one light into the law of kingdoms, the resurrection, and the house of God.

**Thoughts:**
- *★★ The Light of Christ — the law by which all things are governed (88:6-13).* The matter-spectrum / light-and-truth thread's *cosmic fullness*. Christ "descended below all things… that he might be in all and through all things, the light of truth… This is the light of Christ" — and it is *literally everywhere*: "in the sun, and the light of the sun, and the power thereof by which it was made," "in the moon," "the light of the stars," "the earth also" (88:7-10). It is "the light which proceedeth forth from the presence of God to **fill the immensity of space**— the light which is in all things, which giveth life to all things, **which is the law by which all things are governed**" (88:12-13). (The §84:45 chain [truth = light = Spirit = Christ] given its *cosmology*: this light is not a metaphor for moral goodness but the actual *animating and governing substance of the universe* — the power by which the sun shines and the stars were made, the life in all living things, and the *law* by which everything is ordered. The §50:24 "that which is of God is light" reaches its widest scope: the same light that "quickeneth your understandings" [88:11] is the light filling all space and governing all kingdoms. This is the matter-spectrum's deepest claim made *physical* — spirit, light, truth, and law are one reality, Christ's, pervading everything; "he is above all things, and in all things, and is through all things, and is round about all things; and all things are by him, and of him" [88:41]. Panentheism without pantheism: God is *in* all things [the light], yet *above* them [on His throne]. For the project's matter-spectrum thread, §88 is the cosmic counterpart to §84:45 and the setup for §93 — the Light of Christ as the law-bearing, life-giving fabric of reality.)
- *★ The law of the kingdoms — glory is the law you can abide (88:21-40).* The mechanism *under* the §76 three glories. "He who is not able to abide the law of a celestial kingdom cannot abide a celestial glory… he who cannot abide the law of a terrestrial kingdom cannot abide a terrestrial glory" (88:22-24). The glory you inherit is *not* an arbitrary assignment but the *kingdom whose law you are able to live*. (The §76 Vision showed the *three glories*; §88 shows *why* — "all kingdoms have a law given… unto every kingdom is given a law" [88:36-38], and you are drawn to the glory whose law matches what you have *become*. "**That which is governed by law is also preserved by law and perfected and sanctified by the same**" [88:34] — law is not a cage but the *means of perfection*; the same law that governs you preserves, perfects, and sanctifies you. Conversely, "that which… seeketh to become a law unto itself, and willeth to abide in sin… cannot be sanctified by law, neither by mercy, justice, nor judgment… must remain filthy still" [88:35] — the soul that makes *itself* the law forecloses its own sanctification; "filthy still" [Rev 22:11] is self-chosen. And the affinity-law: "intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence; truth embraceth truth; virtue loveth virtue; light cleaveth unto light" [88:40] — like is drawn to like, so you gravitate to the glory you *resemble*. The §76 fitted glory is not imposed; it's *magnetic* — you go where your nature cleaves. For the project: "governed by law → preserved, perfected, sanctified by the same" is the deep grammar — law/order is the means of becoming, not its enemy; and you become what you cleave to.)
- *★ The earth obeys the celestial law and is quickened to glory (88:25-26).* "The earth abideth the law of a celestial kingdom, for it filleth the measure of its creation, and transgresseth not the law— Wherefore, it shall be sanctified; yea, notwithstanding it shall die, it shall be quickened again… and the righteous shall inherit it" (88:25-26). (The earth-transfigured thread [§63:20-21, §77:1 the sea of glass] given its *reason*: the earth *keeps the celestial law* — "transgresseth not the law," fills "the measure of its creation" — and *therefore* is sanctified, dies, is quickened, becomes celestial. The planet is righteous in the only way a planet can be: by perfectly obeying the law of its being. And it will *die and rise* like the body [88:27 — "notwithstanding they die, they also shall rise again, a spiritual body"]: the earth's death-and-quickening mirrors the resurrection. The §76 "begotten sons and daughters" and the celestial bodies now joined by a *celestialized planet*, inherited by "the meek of the earth" [88:17, Matt 5:5]. The matter-spectrum's cosmology complete: the earth is on the same law-keeping road to glory as the soul.)
- *★ "Eye single → filled with light → comprehend all things" (88:67-68).* The personal application of the cosmic light: "if your eye be single to my glory, your whole bodies shall be filled with light… and that body which is filled with light comprehendeth all things" (88:67). (The §4:5 / §82:19 "eye single to the glory of God" given its *result*: a single eye [undivided devotion] lets the §88:12 cosmic light *fill the body*, and "that body which is filled with light **comprehendeth all things**" — understanding is a function of *how much light* you have admitted, which is a function of the *singleness* of your devotion. The matter-spectrum applied to *knowing*: comprehension isn't merely intellectual; it's the body filled with the light of Christ, and the gate is a single eye. "Sanctify yourselves that your minds become single to God, and the days will come that you shall see him; for he will unveil his face" [88:68] — the §67:11/§84:22 capacity-to-see-God thread given its means: sanctification + a single eye → the unveiled face. For the project: understanding follows devotion — the single eye admits the light, and the light-filled mind comprehends; you cannot think your way past a divided heart.)
- *★ The School of the Prophets — "seek learning, even by study and also by faith" + the house of order (88:117-141).* The revelation's practical close founds a *school* — and its charter is the project's own epistemology. "Seek ye out of the best books words of wisdom; **seek learning, even by study and also by faith**" (88:118). (The §9:7-8 study-it-out-then-confirm pattern made a *standing method of learning*: not study *or* faith but study *and* faith — the best books *and* revelation, the intellectual labor *and* the Spirit's confirmation. This is the workspace's exact discipline [the verification epistemology, the source-reading, the curiosity-over-inference] given as a commandment. Then the *house*: "establish a house… of prayer… of fasting… of faith… of learning… of glory… of order, a house of God" [88:119, the temple] — a place where learning and worship are *one*. And the *council order*: "let one speak at a time and let all listen… that when all have spoken that all may be edified of all, and that every man may have an equal privilege" [88:122] — the §50 mutual-edification made a *procedure*: one voice at a time, all listening, all edified, equal privilege [the council pattern the project lives by; not all spokesmen at once]. Plus the practical: "cease to find fault one with another… retire to thy bed early… arise early, that your bodies and your minds may be invigorated" [88:124], and "above all things, clothe yourselves with the bond of charity… the bond of perfectness and peace" [88:125]. The house of God is a house of *order and charity* where one learns by study and faith, speaks in turn, and is edified by all. For the project: §88:118-122 is the charter — study-and-faith learning, the council order, charity above all.)
- *The second Comforter and "draw near unto me" (88:3-5,63).* Two more: the "another Comforter… even the Holy Spirit of promise" [88:3, John 14:16] — the promise of eternal life, "the glory of the celestial kingdom" [88:4]; and the reciprocity of seeking: "**Draw near unto me and I will draw near unto you**; seek me diligently and ye shall find me" [88:63, James 4:8]. (The second Comforter is the §76:53 seal of the Holy Spirit of promise — the ratifying assurance of eternal life [reserved-adjacent]. And the draw-near reciprocity is the relational heart under all the cosmic law: God *responds* to the seeker, drawing near as you draw near — the §6:20 "encircle thee in the arms of my love" at the scale of a lifelong seeking.)
