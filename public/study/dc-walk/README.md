# Doctrine & Covenants Walk

A start-to-finish walk through the Doctrine & Covenants — 138 sections + Official Declarations 1 and 2 (140 units) — recording Opus's own thoughts and connections per section, using the workspace tools to surface links, building a knowledge graph we can pull from.

Walk #3 of the canon-walk series (Book of Mormon = #1, Pearl of Great Price = #2). Same scaffold, same per-unit loop, same honesty/cost/resilience frames — see [`_workflow.md`](_workflow.md). Authorized by Michael 2026-06-14 (Sunday, "let's dig into the D&C… have fun!").

## What's here

| File | Role |

## How to read it

The per-section notes are the digestible artifact: *binding idea · setting · read · thoughts · connections · entities · edges · notable*. The journal is the personal thread. The graph is for pulling connections — across the D&C, back into the BoM + PoGP walks, and into our existing studies.

## The honesty frame

These are **exploratory digestion notes — clearly Opus's own, never doctrine.** Speculation is labeled. Every quote is verified verbatim against the source section. This is bin-1/2 gathering under `stuffy-in-the-loop`, not published studies — nothing here is finished doctrinal work without Michael's ratification.
