## Scripture References

### Direct Miracle Passages
- [D&C 46:21](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/46?lang=eng&id=p21#p21) — "to some is given the working of miracles"
- [1 Corinthians 12:10, 28-29](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/1-cor/12?lang=eng&id=p10#p10) — "working of miracles" listed among gifts and church offices
- [Moroni 10:12](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/10?lang=eng&id=p12#p12) — "to another, that he may work mighty miracles"
- [Mark 16:17-18, 20](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/mark/16?lang=eng&id=p17-p18#p17) — "these signs shall follow them that believe... the Lord working with them, and confirming the word with signs following"

### Faith and Miracles
- [Moroni 7:27-38](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/7?lang=eng&id=p27-p38#p27) — "have miracles ceased? ... Nay ... it is by faith that miracles are wrought" — the essential link between faith and miracles, with the warning that if miracles cease, it's because of unbelief
- [Moroni 10:24-25](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/10?lang=eng&id=p24-p25#p24) — "if the day cometh that the power and gifts of God shall be done away among you, it shall be because of unbelief"
- [Moroni 7:33](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/7?lang=eng&id=p33#p33) — "If ye will have faith in me ye shall have power to do whatsoever thing is expedient in me"

### Healing Miracles (connected to gifts study)
- [Mark 5:25-34](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/mark/5?lang=eng&id=p25-p34#p25) — woman with issue of blood: "Thy faith hath made thee whole"
- [Mark 9:17-29](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/mark/9?lang=eng&id=p17-p29#p17) — casting out unclean spirit, "help thou mine unbelief," "this kind can come forth by nothing, but by prayer and fasting"
- [Acts 14:8-10](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/acts/14?lang=eng&id=p8-p10#p8) — Paul perceiving the crippled man "had faith to be healed"
- [D&C 42:48-51](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/42?lang=eng&id=p48-p51#p48) — "he that hath faith in me to be healed, and is not appointed unto death, shall be healed"

### Angels and Miracles
- [Moroni 7:29-31](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/moro/7?lang=eng&id=p29-p31#p29) — "neither have angels ceased to minister unto the children of men... showing themselves unto them of strong faith"

## Conference Talks
- [Elder Shayne M. Bowen, "Miracles, Angels, and Priesthood Power," April 2024](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/general-conference/2024/04/31bowen?lang=eng) — Patriarch promised "the gift of healing... the dead shall be raised under your hands." Grandfather raised his mother from death. Van accident: Emily brought back to life by priesthood blessing. "President Russell M. Nelson has taught us to seek and expect miracles."

## Threads to Explore
- The relationship between miracles and priesthood power
- Why Mark 9:29 specifies "prayer and fasting" — what does fasting add to faith?
- "Signs follow them that believe" (Mark 16:17) vs. sign-seeking (D&C 46:9) — what's the difference?
- Moroni's warning: if miracles cease, "then has faith ceased also; and awful is the state of man" (Moroni 7:38)
- The Bowen talk pattern: miracles happening according to God's will and timing, not ours
