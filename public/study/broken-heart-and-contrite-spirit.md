# Broken Heart and Contrite Spirit

> *"Ye shall offer for a sacrifice unto me a broken heart and a contrite spirit."*
> — [3 Nephi 9:20](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/9?lang=eng&id=p20#p20)

**Catalyst:** Reading Alma 34 — Amulek's progression from mercy and justice, through praying directives, to charity in verses 27–30. The same arc appears in Enos's prayer. The question: what is it about a broken heart and a contrite spirit that makes this the required sacrifice, and how does this posture bring mercy?

**Binding question:** What do "broken heart" and "contrite spirit" actually mean — and how does this specific posture bring about mercy from the Savior?
