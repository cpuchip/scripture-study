# Focus on Jesus Christ

*For the Young Women — Teaching in the Savior's Way*

---

## The Core Principle

> "No matter what you are teaching, remember that you are really teaching about Jesus Christ and how to become like Him."  
> — [Teach about Jesus Christ No Matter What You Are Teaching](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/manual/teaching-in-the-saviors-way-2022/04-part-1/05-teach-about-jesus-christ?lang=eng)

| **Teach ABOUT Christ** | **Help them COME UNTO Christ** |
|------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Connect to His example, roles, teachings | Help them *feel* His love and want to be like Him |
| *Information* | *Transformation* ← This is where conversion happens |

---

## A Promise for You

> "As you help your learners come to know Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ for themselves, they will be more likely to turn to Them for help, hope, and healing throughout their lives."

**Your teaching matters. The Spirit will help you.**
