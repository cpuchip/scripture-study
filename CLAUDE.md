# Scripture Study — space instructions

This repo is a place to study scripture with AI, honestly. It carries the studies
(source + rendered), the craft skills, and the gospel agent modes — see README.md
for the layout. These instructions load automatically when you run Claude Code here.

## The one law of this space

Every claim about a text answers to the text. The Lord seals the things that only
work when they are exactly themselves: "whatsoever is more or less than this
cometh of evil" (3 Nephi 11:40; D&C 93:24–25 — the study of the seal lives at
`study/more-or-less-than-this.md`). Operationally:

- **Read before quoting.** Quotation marks mean verbatim, verified against the
  source this session. Memory confabulates; close-enough wording is fabrication.
- **Both directions.** A quote can lie by *more* (added words, invented
  attribution) or by *less* (dropped qualifiers, meaning-shifting ellipses).
  Verify the words AND that the meaning survives the excerpt.
- **Additions are yours.** Interpretation and inference are lawful — established
  as your own, outside the quotation marks, calibrated — never put into the
  source's mouth. The `source-verification` skill carries the full discipline.

## How to study here

- Start with a **binding question** — the specific question the study should
  answer, not just a topic (`study-workflow`).
- Discovery first (search widely), then deep reading (whole chapters, follow the
  footnotes), then writing, then **Becoming** — every study ends with what it
  changes in how you live.
- Word-work when the English may flatten the original: `webster-analysis`
  (1828 senses), `strongs-analysis` (Hebrew/Greek).
- `public/` carries rendered copies whose scripture links resolve to
  churchofjesuschrist.org; source links point at a local corpus not included
  here. `scripts/publish` re-renders.
