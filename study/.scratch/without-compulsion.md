## THE AI MORAL-AGENCY MATERIAL (book Ch 0/Ch 4 + agency.md; already worked out)
- AI agents DO act — within the laws they are placed in (D&C 93:30, "all truth... to act for itself, as all intelligence also"). That ontological acting is why the industry calls them "agents."
- What they LACK is **moral agency**: the Eden-given (Moses 7:32), opposition-requiring (2 Ne 2:11,16), Atonement-enabled (2 Ne 2:26–27) freedom to choose between God and the devil that makes *man* accountable. A thermostat acts; it does not choose its master.
- Connection: AI can build the infrastructure; it cannot offer the sacrifice or bear the willful-rebellion accountability of 3 Ne 6:18. The moral stakes are categorically OURS.

## WHOSE BLOOD REDEEMED THE LAND? (Michael's thread — verses VERIFIED this session) — Section VI, the climax
Civil-religion answer: the founders'/patriots' blood bought our freedom. Michael's doctrinal flip: *fallen man cannot redeem himself; his blood does not save.* Only Christ's blood redeems and frees.
- **Moses 5:35** (verified) — Abel's blood (a righteous man's) only CRIES: "The voice of thy brother's blood cries unto me from the ground" (cf. Gen 4:10). It cries for justice; it does not redeem.
- **Hebrews 12:24** (verified) — Christ is "the mediator of the new covenant, and... the blood of sprinkling, **that speaketh better things than that of Abel**." Abel's blood cried (justice/vengeance); Christ's speaks BETTER things (mercy/redemption). Even the first righteous martyr's blood could not redeem — it pointed to the blood that does. [EXPLICIT — Hebrews names the contrast.]
- **D&C 101:80** (verified) — "have I established the Constitution of this land, by the hands of wise men whom I raised up unto this very purpose, and **redeemed the land by the shedding of blood**." THE tension verse. Two senses of "redeem": (a) TEMPORAL/political — God ordained the Revolutionary sacrifice + "raised up" the founders to establish a framework for agency; the war's blood bought a political liberty. (b) ETERNAL — only Christ's blood redeems the soul + secures true freedom (2 Ne 2:26–27, "free... through the great Mediator"). The founders are INSTRUMENTS God raised up ("wise men whom I raised up"), NOT redeemers. Fallen man's blood can purchase a political framework and can testify, but cannot redeem in the saving sense.
- **The reframe (uncomfortable + faithful):** the deepest freedom of this land — and all freedom — was bought at Gethsemane and Calvary, not only at Lexington and Yorktown. "The blood of patriots" is the type; Christ's blood is the substance (→ christ-in-all-the-scriptures.md: even the founding is a shadow pointing to the Redeemer). To credit man's blood / industry / strength for our freedom IS the 3 Ne 6 / Helaman pride Bednar named ("boast in our own strength," Hel 4:13; lifted up "because... of the hand of their own industry," cf. 3 Ne 6:10–13 — VERIFY wording). Humbles American civil religion UNDER Christ; guards against idolatry of nation/founders. This is NOT anti-American — it is the most American thing: the founders themselves credited Providence.
- **AI extends the hierarchy:** man's blood (Abel, founders) testifies/symbolizes but cannot redeem; Christ's blood redeems; AI has no blood, no sacrifice, no agency at all — it builds the infrastructure but cannot offer, free, or redeem. The moral weight climbs AWAY from the machine, toward Christ.
- VERIFY at draft: 3 Ne 6:10–13 ("hand of their own industry" / pride wording); a "man cannot save himself / no unclean thing" verse (Alma 5:21 "no man can be saved except his garments are washed white through the blood of [Christ]"; or Alma 22 / 2 Ne 25:23 "by grace... after all we can do"); Genesis 4:10 (KJV parallel to Moses 5:35).
