# Refinement, Stewardship, and Hope — Source Log

*Working document. Kept as research provenance — traces how observations were reached.*

---

## Binding Question

If the Mechanics of Refinement describe the physical restructuring of spirit-matter to abide glory, how do the three stewardship patterns of the Brother of Jared (Prescription, Rest, and Proposal) and the geometry of Hope (the gaze fixed on the Prototype within the veil) serve as the practical, structural process by which this refinement is achieved? Specifically, how does a steward's active agency (molting stones) interact with the Lord's physical touch to transition from blind obedience to face-to-face seership?

---

## Outline

1. **Introduction / The Confluence of the Three Studies**:
   - Refinement is the *physical goal* (restructuring spirit-matter).
   - Stewardship patterns are the *process/relationship* (how agency interacts with the Lord).
   - Hope is the *attentional mechanics* (what keeps the eye fixed on the Prototype to let the light enter).
2. **The Barges as Spiritual Vessels: Law and Prescription**:
   - Prescription = drilling holes top and bottom.
   - Boundaries of Law (D&C 88:34 - "preserved by law"). If the law is broken, the vessel is flooded (water enters).
   - The physical structures of law are non-negotiable boundaries that make the vessel "tight like unto a dish" and preserve life.
3. **The Winds of Grace: The "I Have Already Done It" Pattern of Rest**:
   - The winds "have gone forth" from the Lord's mouth before the voyage even began.
   - The steward doesn't build a rudder or steer; he enters the vessel, trusts, and sings praises.
   - Passive conducting power, the universal grace of Christ's resurrection and redemptive pull. Hope is anchored to this unseen wind. We rest in the assurance of the Resurrection (Moroni 7:41) and praise Him.
4. **Molting Stones and the Savior's Touch: Active Agency and Proposal**:
   - The steward must act. He molts sixteen stones (transparent glass) from rock. This represents the labor of sanctification—refining one's own desires and choices (grace for grace).
   - The Master touched the stones one by one with His finger. D&C 93:28 says that as a person keeps the commandments, they receive grace for grace and are glorified in truth. The touch of Christ's finger is the literal quickening power (glory) entering the refined matter of the stones.
   - The touch of the finger is physical ("like unto flesh and blood"). The brother of Jared saw it because of his *exceeding faith*.
5. **The Veil, the Eye, and Face-to-Face Seership: The Hinge of Hope**:
   - Connect the tearing of the veil in Ether 3 with the anchor of hope in Hebrews 6 and the single eye in D&C 88:67-68.
   - Seeing the finger of the Lord "according to the flesh" was the moment hope transitioned into sight.
   - The anchor of hope is cast *within the veil*. It pulls us toward the Prototype.
6. **Synthesis and Evidentiary Triplets**:
   - Evidentiary witness law (Deut 19:15, 2 Cor 13:1).
   - Why these three studies form a triplet: Refinement (metaphysics), Stewardship (relationship), and Hope (attention) together form a complete witness of how we become like God.
7. **Open Questions and Refinement Mechanics**:
   - How the resurrection seals capacity, the progress of resurrected substance, the battle for identity, how to know when to shift between prescription and proposal.
8. **Becoming**:
   - Practical commitments.

## Webster 1828

### Refine (verb transitive — genuine 1828, re-verified via webster MCP 2026-06-13)
- **Definition 1:** "To purify; in a general sense; applied to liquors, to depurate; to defecate; to clarify; to separate, as liquor, from all extraneous matter. In this sense, the verb is used with propriety, but it is customary to use fine."
- **Definition 2:** "Applied to metals, to separate the metallic substance from all other matter... to detach the pure metal from all extraneous matter. I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined. Zechariah 13:9."
- **Study relevance:** Purification is structural — the separation of extraneous matter (dross/sin) from the spirit-matter so it can align with celestial law. (CORRECTION: prior draft's "clear from dross or recrement... refine gold or silver" was 1913-style wording; genuine 1828 def 1 is the liquor/depurate sense, and the "refine them as silver" / Zechariah 13:9 text lives at def 2.)

## Gap & Critical Analysis

### Confirmed Strong
- **Transparency Analogy:** The transition of the stones from opaque rock to "transparent glass" via the Brother of Jared's melting (labor) maps perfectly to the purification of spirit-matter under D&C 93 (grace for grace, separating dross). Light of truth requires a transparent medium to shine through.
- **The Wind and Universal Grace:** The wind going forth before the journey aligns with the universal assurance of the resurrection (Moroni 7:41). It is an established, passive force we must rest in.
- **The Evidentiary Triplet:** Metaphysics (D&C 93), Relationship (Stewardship), and Attentional focus (Hope) form a complete three-witness triad (Deut 19:15) establishing the path of exaltation.

### Needs Qualification
- **The Physicality of the Lord's Touch:** The Lord's finger was "like unto flesh and blood," which surprised the Brother of Jared. We must qualify that while Christ's touch quickened the stones physically, this was a pre-mortal manifestation of His physical reality (He had not yet taken upon Him flesh and blood in mortality, but His spirit body was physical fine matter that could be touched and seen by a purified eye).
- **Prescription vs. Law:** We need to clarify that Prescription is the outward embodiment of Law (commandments, ordinances). Just as the hole in the barge was a physical spec, our daily obedience is the physical spec that keeps the water out and lets the air in.

### Acknowledged Gaps
- **The Mechanics of the Touch:** We do not know the exact physics of how pre-mortal spirit-matter (the Lord's finger) interacts with elemental rock to make it glow permanently. We will treat this as an open theological mystery (calibrated confidence).

### Tensions to Name
- **Agency vs. Submission:** The tension between Rest (singing praises while the wind steers) and Proposal (climbing the mountain to molt stones). A steward must navigate when to rest in Christ's finished work and when to labor in active proposal. We will address this tension directly.
