# Truth and the Words of Eternal Life

**Connecting:** [The Mechanics of Existence](./truth.md) + [Christ and the Atonement](./truth-atonement.md) + [Modern Prophets on Truth](./truth-modern-prophets.md) + [Enjoy the Words of Eternal Life](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life.md) + [Enjoy — Reflection](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life-reflection.md)

## The Discovery

Two separate lines of study converged on the same reality.

The [truth study](./truth.md) mapped the equivalence chain from D&C 93:

$$\text{Intelligence} = \text{Light of Truth} = \text{Light and Truth} = \text{Glory of God}$$

The [enjoy reflection](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life-reflection.md) found a parallel chain in D&C 84:44–45:

$$\text{Word} = \text{Truth} = \text{Light} = \text{Spirit of Jesus Christ}$$

And then D&C 88:66 added a third witness:

> My voice is **Spirit**; my Spirit is **truth**; **truth** abideth and hath no end; and if it be in you it shall abound.
> — [D&C 88:66](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)

These are not three different teachings. They are three angles on one ontological reality. Every chain terminates at the same point: truth, light, spirit, intelligence, and the glory of God are descriptions of the *same substance* — the substance of which Christ Himself is composed, and which He transmits to those who receive His words.

This study asks: **What does the mechanics framework reveal about what actually happens when you "enjoy the words of eternal life"?**

## Part 1: Two Chains, One Reality

### The D&C 93 Chain — What You *Are*

The [truth study](./truth.md) traces D&C 93 through a series of identity statements:

> **Intelligence**, or the **light of truth**, was not created or made, neither indeed can be.
> — [D&C 93:29](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)

> The **glory of God** is **intelligence**, or, in other words, **light and truth**.
> — [D&C 93:36](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)

This chain describes the nature of the observer — *you*. At your core, you are intelligence, which is light of truth, which is uncreated, which is the very stuff of God's glory. This is what you fundamentally are.

### The D&C 84 Chain — What the Words *Are*

The [enjoy reflection](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life-reflection.md) traces D&C 84:44–45 through a different chain:

> For the **word of the Lord is truth**, and whatsoever is **truth is light**, and whatsoever is **light is Spirit**, even the Spirit of Jesus Christ.
> — [D&C 84:44–45](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)

This chain describes the nature of the content — *what you receive* when you give heed to the words of eternal life. The word is not information. It is truth, which is light, which is Spirit.

### Where They Meet

The two chains converge because the substance is the same:

| D&C 93 (What You Are) | D&C 84 (What the Words Are) |
|---|---|
| Intelligence = Light of Truth | Word = Truth |
| Light and Truth = Glory of God | Truth = Light |
| Spirit of Truth = Christ | Light = Spirit of Jesus Christ |

You are made of intelligence — light of truth. The words of eternal life transmit truth — which is light, which is Spirit. When you receive the words, you are receiving *the same substance you are made of*, at a higher degree of refinement. Intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence. Light cleaveth unto light. Truth embraceth truth ([D&C 88:40](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)).

This is not metaphor. This is mechanics.

## Part 2: The Words Are Material

### Spirit Is Matter

Joseph Smith's declaration is foundational:

> There is no such thing as immaterial matter. All **spirit is matter**, but it is more fine or pure, and can only be discerned by purer eyes.
> — [D&C 131:7](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/131.md)

### The Words Are Spirit

Jesus declared:

> The **words** that I speak unto you, they **are spirit**, and they **are life**.
> — [John 6:63](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/6.md)

### Therefore

If the words are spirit (John 6:63), and all spirit is matter (D&C 131:7), then the words of eternal life are *material*. Not metaphorically. Not poetically. They are composed of matter "more fine or pure" — the refined substance that the [truth study](./truth.md) placed at the high end of the matter spectrum:

```
Intelligence ←→ Spirit ←→ Physical Element
(purest)         (fine)     (coarse)
```

When you receive the words of eternal life — when you study scripture, hear the voice of the Spirit, receive revelation — something material is being transmitted to you. Truth, which is light, which is spirit, which is fine matter, enters you. This is why D&C 93:28 can describe the process with such mechanical precision:

> He that keepeth his commandments **receiveth truth and light**, until he is glorified in truth and **knoweth all things**.
> — [D&C 93:28](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)

"Receiveth" is not a figure of speech. You literally receive substance — truth and light — and it accumulates in you, grace for grace, until your body is filled with it.

## Part 3: Moses 6:59 — The Nexus

The two study lines converge at the same verse.

### The Enjoy Study Found the Promise

The [enjoy study](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life.md) centered on Moses 6:59 as the *purpose* of the plan of salvation:

> That ye might be sanctified from all sin, and **enjoy the words of eternal life in this world**, and eternal life in the world to come, even immortal glory.
> — [Moses 6:59](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/6.md)

Webster 1828 revealed that "enjoy" means not just pleasure but **possession**: "to have, possess, and use with satisfaction." The words of eternal life are something you can *own*. They are yours to hold, carry, and live from.

### The Truth-Atonement Study Found the Mechanism

The [truth-atonement study](./truth-atonement.md) centered on Moses 6:59–60 as the *mechanics* of rebirth — the three agents by which the Atonement operates:

> Even so ye must be **born again** into the kingdom of heaven, of **water**, and of the **Spirit**, and be cleansed by **blood**, even the blood of mine Only Begotten.
> — [Moses 6:59](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/6.md)

> For by the **water** ye keep the commandment; by the **Spirit** ye are justified, and by the **blood** ye are sanctified.
> — [Moses 6:60](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/6.md)

Water (commandment / obedience), Spirit (justification / truth entering you), Blood (sanctification / the Atonement's purifying power). These are the three agents of refinement — the mechanism by which coarse matter is purified and moved upward on the spectrum.

### Same Verse. Same Breath.

The promise (enjoy the words of eternal life) and the mechanism (born again through water, Spirit, blood) are stated in the *same sentence*. They are inseparable because they are the *same process*:

- Being born again is how you **begin** to enjoy the words of eternal life
- Enjoying the words is how the rebirth **continues**
- The words of eternal life are both the **fruit** of sanctification and the **fuel** of sanctification

The rebirth opens your capacity to receive truth and light. The truth and light refine you further, increasing your capacity. Grace for grace. The circle of existence from the [truth study](./truth.md) is not just an abstract diagram — it is the lived experience of someone who enjoys the words of eternal life:

```
  RECEIVE THE WORDS (truth/light/Spirit)
            ↓
  BORN AGAIN (water/Spirit/blood)
            ↓
  SANCTIFIED (refined on the spectrum)
            ↓
  GREATER CAPACITY to receive more words
            ↓
  RECEIVE MORE (grace for grace)
            ↓
  ...until glorified in truth and knoweth all things
```

## Part 4: The Word Was God

### John's Testimony

John opens his Gospel with the ultimate statement about words:

> In the beginning was the **Word**, and the Word was with God, and the **Word was God**.
> — [John 1:1](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/1.md)

> In him was **life**; and the life was the **light** of men.
> — [John 1:4](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/1.md)

D&C 93 restates this with Restoration clarity:

> In the beginning the **Word** was, for he was the Word, even the messenger of salvation — the **light** and the Redeemer of the world; the **Spirit of truth**.
> — [D&C 93:8–9](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)

The Word is not just *words*. The Word is Christ Himself. He is the light. He is the Spirit of truth. He is "the life of men and the light of men" ([D&C 93:9](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)).

### Peter's Recognition

When Peter declared "thou hast the **words of eternal life**" ([John 6:68](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/6.md)), he was saying more than he knew. The Words of Eternal Life stood before him in the flesh. The words ARE spirit and life (John 6:63) because the Word IS spirit and life. Christ does not merely *speak* truth — He *is* truth. He does not merely *give* light — He *is* light. The words carry His substance because they proceed from His being.

This is why the [truth-atonement study](./truth-atonement.md) established Christ as Refiner, not merely rescuer. He is the light in the system ([D&C 88:6–13](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)). He descended below all things and ascended above all things, comprehending the entire spectrum. The words He speaks are charged with His comprehension — they carry the full weight of the light He IS.

And D&C 88:66 makes the connection explicit: "my **voice** is Spirit; my Spirit is **truth**; truth abideth and hath no end; and **if it be in you it shall abound.**" When God speaks, Spirit enters the listener. That Spirit is truth. That truth abides. It doesn't visit — it *stays*. And if you let it stay, it *abounds* — it grows, expands, fills.

This is what Moses 6:61 describes:

> Therefore it is given **to abide in you**; the record of heaven; the Comforter; the peaceable things of immortal glory; **the truth of all things**; that which quickeneth all things, which maketh alive all things; that which knoweth all things.
> — [Moses 6:61](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/6.md)

Given *to abide in you*. The words of eternal life don't just pass through. They take up residence. They become part of your substance. Truth enters, light grows, the Spirit abides.

## Part 5: The Anti-Enjoy

### D&C 88:32 — The Devastation of Unwillingness

D&C 88 uses the word "enjoy" in a devastating inversion:

> And they who remain shall also be quickened; nevertheless, they shall return again to their own place, **to enjoy that which they are willing to receive, because they were not willing to enjoy that which they might have received**.
> — [D&C 88:32](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)

Read that with the Webster 1828 definition: they were not willing to *have, possess, and use* what was offered. So they receive only what they were willing to possess. The word "enjoy" here carries the full weight of the enjoy study — it is about willingness to *hold and be transformed by* truth and light.

This is the anti-enjoy. The opposite of Moses 6:59's promise. The words of eternal life were available. The substance — truth, light, Spirit, the very matter of God's glory — was offered. And they declined to possess it. Not because they couldn't. Because they *wouldn't*.

### D&C 84:54 — The Darkened Mind

The [enjoy reflection](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life-reflection.md) traced the condemnation warning:

> And your minds in times past have been **darkened** because of unbelief, and because you have **treated lightly** the things you have received.
> — [D&C 84:54](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)

The [truth study](./truth.md) established the asymmetry of light and darkness: "the light shineth in darkness, and the darkness comprehendeth it not" ([John 1:5](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/1.md)). Darkness cannot perceive light. It cannot even recognize what it's missing. This is a structural impossibility — comprehension requires light, and darkness is the absence of it.

Now place that asymmetry alongside D&C 84:54. When the children of Zion treat the words of eternal life lightly — when they have them but don't *enjoy* them — their minds darken. And as their minds darken, they lose the capacity to perceive the value of what they're neglecting. The light withdraws ([D&C 93:37](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md): "Light and truth forsake that evil one"), and what remains is a darkness that *cannot even comprehend what it lost*.

This is why D&C 84:54–57 is so terrifying. The condemnation is not for people who never had the words. It's for people who *had them and didn't realize what they were holding*. Their minds darkened because they treated the words lightly, and that very darkening made it harder to see the value of what they were letting go.

The [Helaman-Ten Virgins study](./helaman-ten-virgins.md) mapped this descent: forget → less astonished → hard → blind → disbelieve → rationalize → "the doctrine of Christ was foolish." Each step is light withdrawing. Each step reduces the capacity to see the next step. The oil runs out not in a single moment but drop by drop by drop.

## Part 6: What Actually Happens When You Study

### The Mechanics of Reception

We can now describe what happens when a person sits down with scripture — *actually* sits down, with the Spirit, and gives diligent heed:

**Step 1: The Word Enters**
You read or hear the word of God. That word is truth ([D&C 84:44](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)). It is not information about truth — it IS truth. "Not created or made, neither indeed can be" ([D&C 93:29](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)).

**Step 2: Truth Becomes Light**
Truth is light ([D&C 84:45](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)). As truth enters, it illuminates. D&C 88:11 describes this: "the light which shineth, which giveth you light, is through him who enlighteneth your eyes, which is the same light that quickeneth your understandings." The *same* light that lets you see physically is the light that quickens your understanding spiritually. One substance, operating at different points on the spectrum.

**Step 3: Light Is Spirit**
Light is the Spirit of Jesus Christ ([D&C 84:45](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)). As light enters, the Spirit enters. This is not sequential — it is simultaneous, because light and Spirit are the same thing. This is why scripture study often *feels* like the Spirit — because it literally IS the Spirit, transmitting through the words.

**Step 4: The Spirit Abides**
If you let it — if you don't treat it lightly — truth abides and "hath no end" ([D&C 88:66](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)). It takes up residence. It becomes part of your substance. Moses 6:61: "it is given to abide in you." You are being materially changed at the finest level of matter.

**Step 5: Grace for Grace**
Each reception of truth and light increases your capacity to receive more. "He that keepeth his commandments receiveth truth and light, until he is glorified in truth and knoweth all things" ([D&C 93:28](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)). The process is incremental. Even Christ "received not of the fulness at first, but continued from grace to grace" ([D&C 93:13](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)).

**Step 6: The Body Filled with Light**
The endpoint:

> And if your eye be single to my glory, your whole bodies shall be **filled with light**, and there shall be no darkness in you; and that body which is filled with light **comprehendeth all things**.
> — [D&C 88:67](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)

A body filled with light is a body that has *fully enjoyed* the words of eternal life. The words have literally become part of the person — truth, light, Spirit received and retained until there is no darkness left. This is the full Webster 1828 enjoy: having, possessing, and using the words of eternal life with complete satisfaction.

And such a body "comprehendeth all things." The [truth study](./truth.md) traced "comprehend" through Webster 1828: to contain, to include, to understand. A body filled with light can *contain* all things because it is made of the same substance — the purest matter, intelligence, light of truth. Intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence.

## Part 7: Zion as Collective Ascent

### The Pattern in Every Dispensation

The [enjoy reflection](./enjoy-the-words-of-eternal-life-reflection.md) traced the Zion pattern across dispensations:

- **Enoch's Zion:** "One heart and one mind, dwelt in righteousness, no poor among them" ([Moses 7:18](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/7.md)). The Lord "dwelt with his people" (v. 16).
- **The Nephite Zion:** "No contention... because of the love of God which did dwell in the hearts of the people... there could not be a happier people" ([4 Nephi 1:15–16](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/4-ne/1.md)). They continued "to hear the word of the Lord" (v. 12).
- **Brigham Young's Vision:** "Here in this house is the Millennium" ([Chapter 45](../gospel-library/eng/manual/teachings-brigham-young/chapter-45.md)).

### What the Mechanics Reveal

The [truth study](./truth.md) established that the matter spectrum runs from coarse to pure, and that the circle of existence describes the upward path: intelligence → law → agency → obedience → truth and light received → glorification. The [truth-atonement study](./truth-atonement.md) identified the Atonement as the engine that powers this ascent after the Fall broke the circuit.

Now apply this to Zion.

Zion is what happens when a *people* collectively ascend the matter spectrum. Each individual receives truth and light through the words of eternal life. Each soul is refined — "quickened by a portion of the celestial glory" ([D&C 88:29](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)). And because intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence, light cleaveth unto light, mercy claimeth her own ([D&C 88:40](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)) — the individual ascent creates a *field effect*. People filled with light draw together. They become "of one heart and one mind" not by committee decision but by shared substance. They are made of the same stuff — truth, light, Spirit — and like recognizes like.

This is why the Nephite Zion had "no contention" — not because they passed good laws, but because "the love of God *did dwell* in the hearts of the people" ([4 Nephi 1:15](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/4-ne/1.md)). The substance of God's love — light, truth, Spirit — was physically present in them. And this is why they continued to "hear the word of the Lord" (v. 12). The word sustained them because the word IS the truth and light and Spirit that composed their unity.

Brigham Young's "here is the Millennium" is not rhetorical enthusiasm. It is mechanical precision. If a people stops serving darkness *right now* — if they resolve to live by the Spirit *right now* — then the Millennium IS present, because the Millennium is the condition in which light governs without opposition. It starts in a house, spreads to a city, covers the earth. Each person enjoying the words of eternal life is a node of light in a growing network of Zion.

### The Reunion

And the endpoint of Zion is breathtaking:

> Then shalt thou and all thy city meet them there, and we will receive them into our bosom, and they shall see us; and **we will fall upon their necks, and they shall fall upon our necks, and we will kiss each other**.
> — [Moses 7:63](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/pgp/moses/7.md)

Enoch's ancient Zion and the latter-day Zion meeting — falling on each other's necks, weeping, embracing. What makes this reunion possible? Both peoples are composed of the same substance. They have been refined by the same process. They enjoy the same words. They are one in the most literal sense — same truth, same light, same Spirit, same glory. Intelligence cleaving unto intelligence across millennia.

## Part 8: D&C 84:19–22 — The Power Behind the Words

### The Key of Knowledge

D&C 84 doesn't just describe the word → truth → light → Spirit chain. It also reveals *why the words of eternal life carry this power*:

> And this greater priesthood administereth the gospel and holdeth the key of the mysteries of the kingdom, even the **key of the knowledge of God**.
>
> Therefore, in the ordinances thereof, the **power of godliness** is manifest.
>
> And without the ordinances thereof, and the authority of the priesthood, the **power of godliness is not manifest** unto men in the flesh;
>
> For without this no man can see the face of God, even the Father, and live.
> — [D&C 84:19–22](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/84.md)

The power of godliness is manifest in the ordinances. The ordinances are administered through the priesthood. The priesthood holds the key of the knowledge of God. And later in the same section: the word is truth is light is Spirit.

The words of eternal life carry power because they are *priesthood-authorized channels* through which the power of godliness flows. This is what Elder Bednar taught: "In the ordinances thereof, the power of godliness is manifest... Ordinances are authorized channels through which the blessings and powers of heaven can flow into our individual lives." The words of eternal life, received in faith through the ordained channels, transmit the actual power of God into the receiver.

This is not bureaucratic structure. This is the mechanics of light transmission. The priesthood holds the "key" — which in the [truth study's](./truth.md) framework means it governs the *law* under which truth and light can be transmitted to mortal beings. Without the key, the power is not manifest. With it, the words carry the fulness.

"Sanctified by the Spirit unto the renewing of their bodies." The Spirit — which IS truth, which IS light, which IS the substance of the words of eternal life — sanctifies *the body*. Not just the mind. Not just the soul abstractly. The *body*. Matter is being refined. The coarse is being purified. The spectrum is being traversed — from telestial to terrestrial to celestial — in the physical body itself.

The [truth-atonement study](./truth-atonement.md) identified this as the culmination: "just men made perfect through Jesus" ([D&C 76:69](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/76.md)) — bodies made celestial, matter refined to the highest glory, the spectrum fully traversed.

And the pathway? Enjoying the words of eternal life. Receiving truth. Being filled with light. Grace for grace. Until:

> Sanctify yourselves that your minds become single to God, and the days will come that **you shall see him**; for he will unveil his face unto you.
> — [D&C 88:68](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88.md)

The promise of Moses 6:59 fulfilled: enjoy the words of eternal life *in this world*, and eternal life — which IS to know the Father and the Son ([John 17:3](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/john/17.md)) — *in the world to come*.

## Synthesis: One Truth, One Process, One Person

The truth study asked: *What is reality made of?*
Answer: Truth, light, intelligence, spirit — all the same substance, at different levels of refinement. Governed by law. Animated by agency. Uncreated.

The truth-atonement study asked: *How does Christ relate to this reality?*
Answer: He IS the light in all things. He descended below all and comprehended all. His Atonement is the engine that powers the upward refinement of fallen beings.

The enjoy study asked: *What does it mean to "enjoy the words of eternal life"?*
Answer: To have, possess, and use — to take into yourself the words that carry eternal life, which are spirit and life.

This study asks: *What is actually happening when all three converge?*

Answer: **The words of eternal life are the delivery mechanism for the substance of God's glory.**

The word is truth. Truth is light. Light is Spirit. Spirit is matter — fine, pure matter. When you enjoy the words of eternal life, you are literally receiving refined matter into your being. That matter is the same substance as intelligence, as glory, as the light of Christ that fills the immensity of space.

The process is sanctification — the refinement of your matter from coarse to pure. The fuel is the words of eternal life. The fire is the Holy Ghost. The Refiner is Christ. The evidence is Zion: a people so filled with light that they become "of one heart and one mind," because they are *composed of the same substance* — truth embracing truth, light cleaving unto light.

And the endpoint is D&C 88:67: a body filled with light, comprehending all things. A being who has fully enjoyed the words of eternal life — who has received, possessed, used, and been transformed by truth until no darkness remains.

That is a celestial being. That is what Moses 6:59 promised. That is what the mechanics describe. That is what Christ makes possible.

> He that keepeth his commandments receiveth truth and light, until he is glorified in truth and knoweth all things.
> — [D&C 93:28](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/93.md)

## Become

1. **Treat scripture study as material reception.** When you open the scriptures, you are not merely reading. You are receiving truth, which is light, which is Spirit. The substance of God's glory enters you through the words. Approach with that awareness.

2. **Stop treating the words lightly.** D&C 84:54 is a warning for covenant people — for *us*. The condemnation is not for rejecting the words but for having them and not realizing what we're holding. Every time you rush through a chapter to check a box, you are turning away from refined matter that was offered to your soul.

3. **Follow the word → truth → light → Spirit chain daily.** D&C 84:46–47 says the Spirit "enlighteneth every man through the world, that hearkeneth to the voice of the Spirit." Hearkening is the key. The words arrive — through scripture, through prayer, through the still small voice. The question is whether you hearken, or treat it lightly.

4. **Recognize that sanctification is physical.** D&C 84:33 says we are "sanctified by the Spirit unto the renewing of their bodies." This is not metaphorical. Your body is being refined by the light you receive. The matter spectrum is real. The sanctification is material. Honor the process.

5. **Build Zion by being filled with light.** You cannot build Zion by organizing programs. You build it by becoming a person filled with truth and light — and finding others who are doing the same. Intelligence cleaveth unto intelligence. Light cleaveth unto light. Zion assembles itself when its members are composed of the right substance.

6. **Remember: the Word is a Person.** John 1:1 — the Word was God. D&C 93:8–9 — He is the Word, the light, the Spirit of truth. When you enjoy the words of eternal life, you are enjoying Christ Himself. Not information about Him. *Him.* The words carry His substance because they proceed from His being. Every moment of genuine scripture study is a moment in His presence.
