# D&C 129 — three grand keys to detect a true messenger

**Setting:** **Nauvoo, Illinois, February 9, 1843** — instruction Joseph gave "making known three grand keys by which the correct nature of ministering angels and spirits may be distinguished." Nine verses: a *test* for any being who claims to bring a message from God. It is the discernment thread the walk has tracked (§28/§50/§52) given its sharpest, most concrete form — a physical protocol for the moment a messenger appears.

**Binding idea:** **"These are three grand keys whereby you may know whether any administration is from God" (129:9).** A resurrected being has flesh and bones — offer your hand and you *feel* it. A just spirit comes in glory and *will not* shake (a just man will not deceive). A devil as an angel of light offers his hand and you feel *nothing* — "you may therefore detect him" (129:8). The test rests on a fixed law: *it is contrary to the order of heaven for a just being to deceive*.

**Read:** "There are two kinds of beings in heaven, namely: Angels, who are resurrected personages, having bodies of flesh and bones— For instance, Jesus said: Handle me and see, for a spirit hath not flesh and bones, as ye see me have. Secondly: the spirits of just men made perfect, they who are not resurrected, but inherit the same glory" (129:1-3). "When a messenger comes saying he has a message from God, offer him your hand and request him to shake hands with you. If he be an angel he will do so, and you will feel his hand" (129:4-5). "If he be the spirit of a just man made perfect he will come in his glory… Ask him to shake hands with you, but he will not move, because it is contrary to the order of heaven for a just man to deceive; but he will still deliver his message" (129:6-7). "If it be the devil as an angel of light, when you ask him to shake hands he will offer you his hand, and you will not feel anything; you may therefore detect him" (129:8). "These are three grand keys whereby you may know whether any administration is from God" (129:9).

**Thoughts:**

- *★ The discernment thread given a physical protocol (129:4-9).* The walk has watched the "is it God or me? / is this true?" question answered structurally (§28 order/stewardship), by edification (§50 the light that edifies), and by a rubric (§52 the five-point pattern). §129 adds a *concrete handshake test* for the rare case of a literal visitant. (The mechanism rests on two fixed laws. First, *the truth of bodies*: a resurrected angel has "flesh and bones" you can *feel* [Luke 24:39, the risen Christ — "handle me and see"]; the counterfeit cannot fake the substance, so "you will not feel anything" [129:8]. Second — and more profound — *the order of heaven forbids a just being to deceive*: the spirit of a just man "will not move… because it is **contrary to the order of heaven for a just man to deceive**" [129:7]; he refuses the handshake *rather than mislead you* into thinking him resurrected. The honesty is built into the *order* itself: a true messenger will not let even a gesture imply a falsehood. For the project's verification covenant and the discernment thread: the deepest test of a source is whether it *refuses to deceive even at small cost* — the just spirit would rather seem unfriendly [refuse the hand] than let you believe something untrue about him; truthfulness reaches down to the handshake. The §128:20 "Michael… detecting the devil when he appeared as an angel of light" given its *method*. Note the §131:7-8 follow-on the footnotes flag: "you will not feel anything" works because even spirit is matter, just "more fine" — the test is physical because the spiritual *is* a refined physical.)

- *Two kinds of glorified beings — the resurrected and the just-made-perfect (129:1-3).* Beneath the test is a taxonomy: heaven holds *resurrected* personages (flesh and bones, like the risen Christ) *and* "the spirits of just men made perfect… not resurrected, but inherit the same glory" (129:3, Heb 12:23). (The matter-spectrum's anthropology: glory comes in *both* an embodied and a spirit form [the embodied higher, §130:22 forward]; the just dead, awaiting resurrection, already "inherit the same glory." The taxonomy is what makes the test *work* — knowing the two true kinds lets you spot the third, false kind. For the walk's matter-spectrum thread: spirit and resurrected body are points on one continuum of glory, not different categories.)
