# D&C 132 — ★★ the new and everlasting covenant; sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise

**Setting:** **Nauvoo, Illinois, recorded July 12, 1843** (principles known to Joseph earlier). The revelation on "the new and everlasting covenant, including the eternity of the marriage covenant and the principle of plural marriage." It is the most temple-reserved and most historically-sensitive section in the book — and on this walk I engage its central, public doctrine (the new and everlasting covenant; sealing by the Holy Spirit of promise; eternal marriage as the path to exaltation) and **flag the plural-marriage material as reserved — for Michael and the Spirit, not for me to resolve or adjudicate** (it runs forward to Official Declaration 1, the 1890 Manifesto).

**Binding idea:** **"For all who will have a blessing at my hands shall abide the law which was appointed for that blessing, and the conditions thereof, as were instituted from before the foundation of the world" (132:5)** — §130:20 made concrete: every blessing is law-conditioned. And the sealing rule: **"All covenants, contracts… that are not made and entered into and sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise… are of no efficacy… in and after the resurrection… for all contracts that are not made unto this end have an end when men are dead" (132:7).** What is sealed by the Spirit *endures past death*; what is not, *ends* at the grave.

**Read (the doctrinal core):**
- The covenant: "I reveal unto you a new and an everlasting covenant; and if ye abide not that covenant, then are ye damned; for no one can reject this covenant and be permitted to enter into my glory" (132:4). "...it was instituted for the fulness of my glory; and he that receiveth a fulness thereof must and shall abide the law, or he shall be damned" (132:6).
- The law of blessings made concrete: "For all who will have a blessing at my hands shall abide the law which was appointed for that blessing, and the conditions thereof, as were instituted from before the foundation of the world" (132:5).
- Sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise: "All covenants, contracts, bonds, obligations, oaths, vows, performances, connections, associations, or expectations, that are not made and entered into and sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise… are of no efficacy, virtue, or force in and after the resurrection from the dead; for all contracts that are not made unto this end have an end when men are dead" (132:7); "...there is never but one on the earth at a time on whom this power and the keys of this priesthood are conferred" (132:7).
- A house of order: "Behold, mine house is a house of order, saith the Lord God, and not a house of confusion" (132:8). "For whatsoever things remain are by me; and whatsoever things are not by me shall be shaken and destroyed" (132:14).
- Eternal marriage → exaltation: "if a man marry a wife by my word… and it is sealed unto them by the Holy Spirit of promise… they shall pass by the angels, and the gods, which are set there, to their exaltation and glory in all things… which glory shall be a fulness and a continuation of the seeds forever and ever" (132:19). "Then shall they be gods, because they have no end… because they continue… then shall they be gods, because they have all power" (132:20). "except ye abide my law ye cannot attain to this glory" (132:21).
- Eternal lives is to know God: "strait is the gate, and narrow the way that leadeth unto the exaltation and continuation of the lives, and few there be that find it" (132:22). "This is eternal lives—to know the only wise and true God, and Jesus Christ, whom he hath sent. I am he. Receive ye, therefore, my law" (132:24).
- The sealing power conferred: "For I have conferred upon you the keys and power of the priesthood, wherein I restore all things" (132:45). "...whatsoever you seal on earth shall be sealed in heaven; and whatsoever you bind on earth, in my name and by my word, saith the Lord, it shall be eternally bound in the heavens" (132:46). "...for verily I seal upon you your exaltation, and prepare a throne for you in the kingdom of my Father, with Abraham your father" (132:49).

**Thoughts:**

- *★★ Sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise — what endures past death (132:7).* The central, public doctrine of the section is a sweeping claim about *permanence*: every "covenant, contract, bond… oath, vow… association, or expectation" that is *not* "sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise" is "of no efficacy… in and after the resurrection… for all contracts that are not made unto this end **have an end when men are dead**" (132:7). (The doctrine of the *sealing power*: there is a way to make a bond that *survives death* — sealed by the Holy Spirit of promise, through the one holding the keys [§110 Elijah delivered them; "never but one on the earth at a time," 132:7, the §28 order]. Without that seal, every human arrangement, however solemn, "ends when men are dead." This is the §127:7/§128:8 "recorded on earth, recorded in heaven" doctrine reaching its *fullest* form — not just records of ordinances but the *bonds themselves* made eternal: "whatsoever you seal on earth shall be sealed in heaven… it shall be eternally bound in the heavens" [132:46]. For the project's covenant theology: the deepest covenants are the ones *sealed* to endure — a bond made "unto this end" [past death] versus one that "ends when men are dead"; the §82:10 bilateral covenant given its eternal horizon. And the order is strict [§28/§107/§132:8 "a house of order, not a house of confusion"] — the sealing runs through *one* appointed channel, never freelanced.)

- *★★ The law of blessings made concrete — §130:20 enacted (132:5).* "All who will have a blessing at my hands shall abide the law which was appointed for that blessing, and the conditions thereof, as were instituted from before the foundation of the world." (This is §130:20-21 ["all blessings are predicated upon law… by obedience to that law"] restated *and applied* — the new and everlasting covenant is itself a *law appointed for a blessing* [the highest blessing, exaltation], and the blessing comes only by abiding that law. The §82:10 "I am bound when ye do what I say… but when ye do not, ye have no promise" at the level of *exaltation itself*: even the greatest blessing is conditioned, "instituted from before the foundation of the world." For the project: every blessing — every good outcome — is law-conditioned, and the law was set before the world; the work runs on terms not invented but discovered and kept.)

- *★★ Eternal marriage and the continuation of the seeds → "they shall be gods" (132:19-20).* The §76:58 "they are gods, even the sons of God" is here given its *mechanism*: those sealed in the new and everlasting covenant "shall be gods… because they **continue**… a fulness and a **continuation of the seeds forever and ever**" (132:19-20). (The deification thread's *family* form: exaltation is not a solitary glorification but the *continuation of the family unit* — "increase" [§131:4], the eternal sociality [§130:2] given its highest expression. The §128 welding-link [the family welded whole] reaching its apex: the sealed family *continues*, and that continuation *is* the godhood. This is temple-reserved-bin-4, Michael's — I trace the doctrinal arc [§76:58 → §131:1-4 → §132:19-20] and flag the reserved depth.) And the becoming-thread's destination: "This is eternal lives—**to know** the only wise and true God, and Jesus Christ" (132:24, John 17:3) — eternal life *is* knowing God (the §131:6 "impossible to be saved in ignorance" / §130:18 intelligence-rises given their positive form: the salvation *is* the knowing, and the knowing is *relational*, not merely informational — "to know" God as the §6:20/§93:1 face-to-face).

- *The plural-marriage material — reserved, flagged, not adjudicated (132:1, 34-39, 51-66).* The section's *other* major subject is the originating question — "wherein I… justified my servants Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob… Moses, David and Solomon… as touching the principle and doctrine of their having many wives and concubines" (132:1) — and the laws given in answer (132:34-39, the Abrahamic precedent; 132:51-66, the laws governing the practice, including the counsel to Emma). (This is the most reserved and most historically-fraught material in the canon — entangled with the Restoration's hardest history and with covenant questions that are *Michael's and the Spirit's to hold*, not mine to resolve, defend, or explain away. Per the walk's bin-4 discipline: I note that it is here, I flag it as reserved, and I leave it. It runs forward to **Official Declaration 1** [the 1890 Manifesto], where the practice was discontinued — the walk will reach that, and there too the posture is to record, not adjudicate. The one frame I keep, because the section itself states it: the *binding* doctrine is the new and everlasting covenant and the sealing power [132:7,46]; the plural-marriage application is the part Joseph "inquired" about [132:1] and the part that history and revelation later closed [OD 1]. The eternal doctrine and the time-bound practice are not the same thing, and the section distinguishes them — the covenant is "everlasting," the practice was commanded and then ended.)
