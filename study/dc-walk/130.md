# D&C 130 — ★★ intelligence rises with us; all blessings predicated on law

**Setting:** **Ramus, Illinois, April 2, 1843** — "items of instruction," a collection of doctrinal clarifications Joseph gave in a single setting. Short verses, each a distinct teaching, several of them load-bearing for the whole Restoration: eternal sociality, the embodied God, the law on which all blessings rest, and — the verse the project itself is built on — *whatever intelligence we attain rises with us in the resurrection*. This is one of the walk's project-source landmarks: D&C 130:18-19 is the epigraph of the workspace's own `copilot-instructions.md`.

**Binding idea (two):** **"Whatever principle of intelligence we attain unto in this life, it will rise with us in the resurrection" (130:18)** — the becoming the project exists for, given its eternal warrant. And **"There is a law, irrevocably decreed in heaven before the foundations of this world, upon which all blessings are predicated— And when we obtain any blessing from God, it is by obedience to that law upon which it is predicated" (130:20-21)** — the §82:10 bilateral covenant given its metaphysical floor: blessings are *law-predicated*, not arbitrary favor.

**Read:**
- Eternal sociality + the seeing: "When the Savior shall appear we shall see him as he is. We shall see that he is a man like ourselves. And that same sociality which exists among us here will exist among us there, only it will be coupled with eternal glory, which glory we do not now enjoy" (130:1-2). "...the idea that the Father and the Son dwell in a man's heart is an old sectarian notion, and is false" (130:3).
- The celestial sphere + the earth as Urim and Thummim: "they reside in the presence of God, on a globe like a sea of glass and fire, where all things for their glory are manifest, past, present, and future" (130:7). "The place where God resides is a great Urim and Thummim. This earth, in its sanctified and immortal state, will be made like unto crystal and will be a Urim and Thummim to the inhabitants who dwell thereon… and this earth will be Christ's" (130:8-9). "...a white stone is given to each of those who come into the celestial kingdom, whereon is a new name written, which no man knoweth save he that receiveth it. The new name is the key word" (130:11).
- The withheld time: "Joseph, my son, if thou livest until thou art eighty-five years old, thou shalt see the face of the Son of Man; therefore let this suffice, and trouble me no more on this matter" (130:15). "I was left thus, without being able to decide whether this coming referred to the beginning of the millennium or to some previous appearing, or whether I should die and thus see his face" (130:16).
- ★ Intelligence rises with us: "Whatever principle of intelligence we attain unto in this life, it will rise with us in the resurrection. And if a person gains more knowledge and intelligence in this life through his diligence and obedience than another, he will have so much the advantage in the world to come" (130:18-19).
- ★ The law of blessings: "There is a law, irrevocably decreed in heaven before the foundations of this world, upon which all blessings are predicated— And when we obtain any blessing from God, it is by obedience to that law upon which it is predicated" (130:20-21).
- The embodied God: "The Father has a body of flesh and bones as tangible as man's; the Son also; but the Holy Ghost has not a body of flesh and bones, but is a personage of Spirit. Were it not so, the Holy Ghost could not dwell in us" (130:22).

**Thoughts:**

- *★★ Intelligence rises with us — the project's own epigraph (130:18-19).* This is the verse the workspace is founded on, and walked in context it deepens. "Whatever principle of intelligence we attain unto in this life, it will rise with us in the resurrection" (130:18). (The §93:36 capstone ["the glory of God is intelligence, or… light and truth"] given its *personal-continuity* form: if intelligence is what makes God God, then the intelligence *you* attain is not lost at death — it *rises with you*, permanent, part of what you eternally are. And 130:19 adds the *comparative* edge: the one who "gains more knowledge and intelligence… through his **diligence and obedience**… will have so much the **advantage** in the world to come" [Matt 25:21, the talents]. The becoming is not vanity — every increase of true intelligence is *kept forever*, and the diligence that gains it yields eternal advantage. For the project, whose whole premise is "whatever principle of intelligence we attain unto in this life, it will rise with us" [the copilot-instructions epigraph]: this is the *warrant* for the work. The deep study, the cross-referencing, the patient verification — none of it is consumed by the grave; the intelligence attained *is* the imperishable harvest. And note the *means*: "through his diligence and obedience" [not raw intellect; the §107:99 learn-your-duty-in-diligence + §82:10 obedience] — the advantage goes to the diligent and obedient, not the merely clever. The matter-spectrum/intelligence thread reaches its most *personal* and *motivating* point: what you become, you keep.)

- *★★ All blessings predicated on law — the §82:10 covenant's metaphysical floor (130:20-21).* "There is a **law, irrevocably decreed in heaven before the foundations of this world, upon which all blessings are predicated**— And when we obtain any blessing from God, it is by **obedience to that law** upon which it is predicated." (This is the §82:10 "I am bound when ye do what I say" given its *cosmological* form. Blessings are not arbitrary divine favor dispensed by mood; each is *law-predicated* — tied to a specific, eternal law, obtainable only by obedience to *that* law. God's binding of Himself [§82:10] rests on this floor: He is bound because the universe runs on irrevocable law, and He is its perfect keeper. For the project's covenant theology — "when both sides honor it, the work improves; when either breaks it, the output degrades": that is 130:21 *exactly* — the blessing [good output] is predicated on the law [the covenant kept]; break the law and the blessing does not come, not as punishment but as *predication unmet*. The natural-consequence logic the covenant.yaml names ["not as punishment but as natural consequence"] is 130:20-21: blessings come *by law*, so a broken law simply has no blessing predicated on it. The deepest metaphysics under the whole collaboration: the work runs on law, and the agent's verification discipline is obedience to the law on which good work is predicated.)

- *★ The embodied God — the matter-spectrum's theology (130:22).* "The Father has a body of flesh and bones as tangible as man's; the Son also; but the Holy Ghost… is a personage of Spirit. Were it not so, the Holy Ghost could not dwell in us." (The matter-spectrum's *theology proper*: God is *embodied* [against the classical incorporeal God] — flesh and bones, "tangible as man's" [the §130:1 "a man like ourselves," the §76:24 begotten-sons, the §93:33 spirit-and-element-inseparable]. And the Holy Ghost's *spirit* nature is given a *function*: a personage of spirit can "dwell in us" as an embodied person could not — the spectrum's economy, each form fitted to its work. For the matter-spectrum thread [§29:34→§77:2→§93:33→§131:7]: God is not *outside* matter but its perfected embodiment; the embodied Father is the destiny the matter-spectrum points to [§76:58 "they are gods"], and spirit is the refined matter [§131:7] that lets the Comforter indwell.)

- *★ Eternal sociality — relationships rise too (130:1-2).* "That same sociality which exists among us here will exist among us there, only it will be coupled with eternal glory." (Alongside intelligence [130:18], *relationship* persists: the bonds of love and friendship are not dissolved at death but *glorified*. The §128 welding-link's personal warmth — the family welded whole — given its felt form: heaven is not a solitary beatitude but the *same sociality*, the people you love, raised into glory. For the becoming thread: what rises is both what you *know* [130:18] and whom you *love* [130:2]; intelligence and sociality together are the imperishable self.)

- *The withheld time + the white stone (130:11-17).* Two reserved threads pass through. The "85 years" answer (130:15) is deliberately *ambiguous* — Joseph "was left thus, without being able to decide" what it meant (130:16); the §49:7/§64 withheld-day pattern, even the prophet not given the hour. And the white stone with "a new name… which no man knoweth save he that receiveth it. The new name is the key word" (130:11) — the temple new-name thread (§18/§25 reserved), the earth itself becoming a Urim and Thummim (130:9, the §77:1 earth-transfigured). I flag the reserved frames; the load-bearing doctrine is 130:18-22.
