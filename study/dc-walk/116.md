# D&C 116 — Adam-ondi-Ahman: where Adam shall come to visit his people

**Setting:** **Near Wight's Ferry, at a place called Spring Hill, Daviess County, Missouri, May or June 1838** — an extract from Joseph Smith's history. As the Saints settled the area, the Lord renamed a hill, identifying it as the site of a great future gathering.

**Binding idea:** "Spring Hill is named by the Lord Adam-ondi-Ahman, because… it is the place where Adam shall come to visit his people, or the Ancient of Days shall sit, as spoken of by Daniel the prophet" (116:1). A frontier hill in Missouri is named as the place of *Daniel's* vision — where "the Ancient of Days" (Adam) will sit, and Adam will come to visit his people.
