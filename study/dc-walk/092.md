# D&C 92 — you shall be a lively member

**Setting:** **Kirtland, Ohio, March 15, 1833.** A two-verse revelation instructing the United Firm (the united order, §78/§82) to receive **Frederick G. Williams** — newly appointed Joseph's counselor (§81/§90) — into the order, and charging Williams himself.

**Binding idea:** "You shall be a **lively member** in this order; and inasmuch as you are faithful in keeping all former commandments you shall be blessed forever" (92:2). Membership in the order is not passive enrollment but *lively* participation — an active, contributing member.

**Read:** "I give unto the united order… a revelation and commandment concerning my servant Frederick G. Williams, that ye shall receive him into the order. What I say unto one I say unto all" (92:1). "You shall be a lively member in this order; and inasmuch as you are faithful in keeping all former commandments you shall be blessed forever" (92:2).

**Thoughts:**
- *★ "A lively member" — active, not passive (92:2).* The single charge to Williams is to be *lively* in the order — and the adjective carries the whole. (The §58:29 anti-passivity ["he that doeth not anything until he is commanded… is a slothful servant"] at the scale of *membership*: to be received into the order is not to hold a seat but to *live* in it — to contribute, to be "anxiously engaged" [§58:27]. The §84:109-110 body-of-Christ ["the body hath need of every member"] given a verb: a *lively* member is one the body actually *needs*, working its part. And the condition — "inasmuch as you are faithful in keeping all former commandments" [92:2] — ties the new calling to *prior* faithfulness [§82:10 the bilateral covenant; the blessing flows when the keeping continues]. For any council or order the project knows: membership is *lively participation*, not a passive seat; the body needs members who *act*.)
- *"What I say unto one I say unto all" (92:1).* The recurring universalizer [§61:18, §82:5, §93:49]: the word to Williams is the word to *every* member of the order. (The specific call is a *general* pattern — every member is to be *lively*. The §25:16 "this is my voice unto all" mechanism: a word to one steward is the standing instruction to all in the same stewardship.)
