**Z.ai Launches GLM-5.2 for Long-Horizon AI Tasks**
Z.ai has released GLM-5.2, an open-source flagship model (MIT license) designed for project-scale engineering and long-horizon planning. The model features a 1 million-token context window and is specifically optimized to support complex agentic workflows and coding-agent trajectories that require sustained reasoning across large amounts of context.
