## Scripture References

### Direct Miracle Passages
- [D&C 46:21](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/46.md) — "to some is given the working of miracles"
- [1 Corinthians 12:10, 28-29](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/1-cor/12.md) — "working of miracles" listed among gifts and church offices
- [Moroni 10:12](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/moro/10.md) — "to another, that he may work mighty miracles"
- [Mark 16:17-18, 20](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/mark/16.md) — "these signs shall follow them that believe... the Lord working with them, and confirming the word with signs following"

### Faith and Miracles
- [Moroni 7:27-38](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/moro/7.md) — "have miracles ceased? ... Nay ... it is by faith that miracles are wrought" — the essential link between faith and miracles, with the warning that if miracles cease, it's because of unbelief
- [Moroni 10:24-25](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/moro/10.md) — "if the day cometh that the power and gifts of God shall be done away among you, it shall be because of unbelief"
- [Moroni 7:33](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/moro/7.md) — "If ye will have faith in me ye shall have power to do whatsoever thing is expedient in me"

### Healing Miracles (connected to gifts study)
- [Mark 5:25-34](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/mark/5.md) — woman with issue of blood: "Thy faith hath made thee whole"
- [Mark 9:17-29](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/mark/9.md) — casting out unclean spirit, "help thou mine unbelief," "this kind can come forth by nothing, but by prayer and fasting"
- [Acts 14:8-10](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/acts/14.md) — Paul perceiving the crippled man "had faith to be healed"
- [D&C 42:48-51](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/42.md) — "he that hath faith in me to be healed, and is not appointed unto death, shall be healed"

### Angels and Miracles
- [Moroni 7:29-31](../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/bofm/moro/7.md) — "neither have angels ceased to minister unto the children of men... showing themselves unto them of strong faith"

## Conference Talks
- [Elder Shayne M. Bowen, "Miracles, Angels, and Priesthood Power," April 2024](../gospel-library/eng/general-conference/2024/04/31bowen.md) — Patriarch promised "the gift of healing... the dead shall be raised under your hands." ## Threads to Explore
- The relationship between miracles and priesthood power
- Why Mark 9:29 specifies "prayer and fasting" — what does fasting add to faith?
- "Signs follow them that believe" (Mark 16:17) vs. sign-seeking (D&C 46:9) — what's the difference?
- Moroni's warning: if miracles cease, "then has faith ceased also; and awful is the state of man" (Moroni 7:38)
- The Bowen talk pattern: miracles happening according to God's will and timing, not ours
