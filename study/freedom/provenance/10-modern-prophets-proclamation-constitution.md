## 3. The "great and dreadful day of the Lord" [ESTABLISHED]
- **Malachi 4:5** — "before the coming of the great and dreadful day of the Lord."
- **JS-History 1:36-39** — Moroni's variant: v.38 "I will reveal unto you the Priesthood, by the hand of Elijah the prophet, before... the great and dreadful day"; v.37 the proud "shall burn as stubble" vs the promise to the children — the great/dreadful split in one quotation.
- **D&C 2** (Sept 1823) + **D&C 110:13-16** (Kirtland, Apr 3 1836): v.16 "the great and dreadful day of the Lord is **near, even at the doors**."
- The **great (righteous) / dreadful (wicked)** dual valence of the SAME day is textually grounded (ties to provenance/08 Mal 4:1-2 sorting).
