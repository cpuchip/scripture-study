## PART 1 — The warrant: the BoM was written FOR US [ESTABLISHED text; INTERPRETIVE-faithful conclusion]
- **Mormon 8:34–35** (Moroni) — "I speak unto you as if ye were present, and yet ye are not. But behold, **Jesus Christ hath shown you unto me, and I know your doing.**" The study's "Moroni saw us" is the TEXT'S OWN CLAIM, not imposed.
- **1 Nephi 19:23** — "I did liken all scriptures unto us." The book's own hermeneutic.
- **2 Nephi 25:23–26** — "we labor diligently to write, to persuade our children... to believe in Christ... that our children may know to what source they may look for a remission of their sins." (Prophetic warrant — Benson 1986 "Keystone," Bednar Oct 2024 — held by lead.)

## PART 2 — The Nephite cycle around the FIRST coming as a type [beats ESTABLISHED; type INTERPRETIVE-faithful]
- **3 Nephi 6:12** — "distinguished by **ranks**, according to their riches and their chances for learning." (inequality)
- **3 Nephi 6:18** — "they did not sin ignorantly... therefore they did **wilfully rebel** against God."
- **3 Nephi 6:27–30** — secret combination "administered by the devil"; v.30 (GOLD): "covenant one with another to destroy the governor, and to establish a king over the land, **that the land should no more be at liberty but should be subject unto kings.**"
- **3 Nephi 7:2–6** — "divided one against another... into **tribes**... thus they did destroy the government of the land"; "because of the **secret combination**."
- **3 Nephi 8:5–7, 23** — storm/tempest/earthquake at the crucifixion; "**three days** that there was no light seen."
- **Helaman 14:20–27** (Samuel, ~6 BC) — prophesies the death-signs incl. three days darkness, earthquakes, "highways broken," cities desolate. → **3 Nephi 1:15–21** birth-sign fulfilled exactly (unbelievers had set a DATE to execute believers if it failed — frustrated). → **3 Nephi 8** death-signs fulfilled. **The book MODELS that signs precede Christ's coming and fulfill to the letter** — strong internal warrant for the type.
- **3 Nephi 11** — Christ appears (AD 34). **4 Nephi 1:2–3** — Zion: "all made free," all things common, no rich/poor/bond/free. **4 Nephi 1:22–24** — pride re-enters year 201. **Mormon 6:5–6** — final destruction, AD 385.

## PART 4 — Revelation 12 [ESTABLISHED text; INTERPRETIVE application]
- **Rev 12:6** — "the woman fled into the wilderness, where she hath a place prepared of God... a thousand two hundred and threescore days."
- **Rev 12:14** — "two wings of a great eagle, that she might fly into the wilderness, into her place, where she is nourished... from the face of the serpent." → maps onto the Church's flight to the Great Basin (1846–47); reader discerns.

## STRONGEST parallels (defensible)
1. **3 Ne 6:30 "no more at liberty but subject unto kings"** → loss of self-government / consolidation of power. The richest freedom-verse; uses the book's own liberty vocabulary. [strong]
2. **Inequality "by ranks" (3 Ne 6:12)** as the leading indicator of dissolution (and the thing that ENDS Zion, 4 Ne 1:24). [strong]
3. **Division into tribes/faction (3 Ne 7:2–6)** → social fragmentation dissolving a shared body politic. [strong]
4. **Prophesied signs fulfilled exactly (Hel 14 → 3 Ne 1, 8)** → strong internal warrant that Christ's coming is preceded by named, literally-fulfilled signs. [strong]
5. **Rev 12 woman → Church's flight to a prepared wilderness.** [moderate–strong]

## HONEST WEAKEST / FORCED parallels (WHATEVER THE TRUTH BRINGS — the critical-analysis gold)
1. **"~3 years before Christ appeared" is LOOSE.** Collapse = AD 26–30; destruction (3 Ne 8) AND appearance (3 Ne 11) are BOTH AD 34. So it's ~4 yrs from the end of the collapse window to AD 34, and destruction+appearance are the SAME year (appearance follows destruction by months). Do NOT say "3 years before the appearance" as a tidy gap. (Corrects Michael's "3 years before the nation breaks up.")
2. **Rev 12's numbers don't fit Utah.** 1,260 days / "time, times, half a time" (~3.5 yrs) — the Saints were in the Great Basin far longer. The flight MOTIF maps; the DURATION does not. Resonant type of protected flight, NOT a dated prophecy.
3. **★ THE FORK — "which remnant are we?"** The Nephite collapse ends in national EXTINCTION (Mormon 6); the Church's flight ends in PRESERVATION + statehood. If we map the latter-day reader onto the Nephite NATION, the type points to destruction; if onto the righteous REMNANT who receive Christ, it points to Zion. **TWO different typological assignments — do NOT silently switch.** The study must NAME which remnant the reader is being likened to. (This is the keystone critical insight: the type is doom OR hope depending on which remnant we choose to be — which IS the agency point, and the faithful answer to "how bad does it look?")
4. **"Secret combination → king" is monarchy-specific.** Generalizing "kings" to modern non-monarchical tyranny is reasonable but requires the reader to generalize; note the text's specific target.
5. **The ~200-yr Zion (4 Ne) had no external persecution** — it was undone from WITHIN (pride, 4 Ne 1:24), not a fugitive society. The "flight to safety" type (Rev 12 / Utah) and the "Zion achieved" type (4 Ne) are different shapes; don't weld them into one arc.
