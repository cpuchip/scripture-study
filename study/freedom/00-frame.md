## Binding question

**Sub-questions:**
1. Whose blood frees a land — and a soul? (the doctrinal spine: Cain/Abel, "without compulsion," D&C 101:80 vs. Hebrews 12:24)
2. Why does no earthly party hold "maximum agency," and is national breakup the scriptural expectation (D&C 87:6; Ether 2)?
3. What in our history were *real* losses of freedom (verified), and what is faithful interpretation vs. speculation?
4. How "near" is near — and how do we read signs without setting dates?
5. How do we serve the God of this land — who is Jesus Christ — and prepare our house regardless of the nation?

## GOVERNING GUARDRAILS (read before adding anything to this folder)

This study is charged — end-times, national collapse, political claims, "signs." The frame is what keeps it faithful rather than conspiratorial. Every contributor (human, subagent, substrate) holds these:

1. **Whatever the truth brings.** This is discovery, not confirmation of a thesis. Follow the sources even when they complicate or overturn the starting intuition. If a sign is folk-speculation, say so. If a historical claim is one of several scholarly readings, present the range. If "inevitable" is really "conditional," honor the condition.

2. **Three-tier labeling — mark every claim.** (Inherited from the [christ-in-all-the-scriptures](../christ-in-all-the-scriptures.md) §VII discipline.)
   - **[ESTABLISHED]** — verifiable fact: scripture text (gospel_get), prophetic statements (read the talk), dated history + amendment texts + court holdings (official/primary source + URL), eclipse paths (NASA). Multi-source where contested.
   - **[INTERPRETIVE — faithful]** — type/shadow readings and likenings. The BoM-as-blueprint-for-our-day is *prophet-warranted* (Benson "Keystone"; Bednar "In the Space of Not Many Years") — strong ground. Specific parallels (Nephite cycle ↔ U.S./Church) are faithful application the reader discerns, not proof.
   - **[SPECULATIVE — discern]** — folk/personal readings: eclipse-as-sign, "we are the Empire," any sense of nearness. Engaged honestly, never dressed as doctrine. Michael's Spirit is the final filter.

3. **The date-stamp line (hard).** We do NOT set or imply a date for the Second Coming or for collapse. "Of that day and hour knoweth no man" ([Matthew 24:36](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/nt/matt/24.md)); "the hour and the day no man knoweth" ([D&C 49:7](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/49.md)). We read signs as the prophets do — *watch, be ready, discern the season* — never a clock. The spine study's rule stands: "the Lord has not given any of us authority to date-stamp."

4. **Non-partisan; repentance, not politics.** The doctrine indicts every party and, first, *us*. Michael: "republican/democrat/libertarian — they all fail here." The Empire/Palpatine frame is a mirror aimed at ourselves (we who assume we are the rebels), not a weapon for a side. Keep it a call to serve Christ, not a political program.

5. **Conditional, not fatalist (the hope).** The land's promise is conditional (Ether 2:8–12): serve the God of the land or be swept off. Collapse follows willful rebellion (3 Ne 6:18); repentance can stay it; and regardless of the nation, the covenant house can be secured — "stand in holy places, and be not moved" ([D&C 87:8](../../gospel-library/eng/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/87.md)). The study ends in agency and hope, not doom.

6. **Source verification ironclad.** Every fact: official/primary source + URL, multiple sources where contested, multiple viewpoints on interpretive history. Every scripture: gospel_get. Every prophet quote: read the talk. Provenance saved per topic under `study/freedom/provenance/`. Where a tool falls short, escalate to pg-ai-stewards `deep_research` or the `agy -p` skill — but ground everything in truth.

7. **The center is Jesus Christ, the God of this land.** Not America-worship, not doom-watching. The whole resolves to Him — the blood that actually frees, the God the land must serve, the source of the only freedom that survives a nation's fall.
