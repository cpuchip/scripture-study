## Framework for Evaluation

From the priestcraft study, three diagnostic questions:

1. **Who is the light?** — Does this person point to Christ, or position themselves as the source of insight?
2. **What is the motive?** — Is the content shaped by the Spirit or by the algorithm? Would they do this without monetization?
3. **Who benefits?** — Does the audience come away closer to Christ and the scriptures, or more dependent on this creator?

Additional signals to watch for:
- Do they claim special authority or revelation beyond what the prophets teach?
- Do they create urgency/fear to drive engagement?
- Is the content substantive or sensational?
- Do they submit to or subtly undermine living prophets and apostles?
- What's the ratio of "buy my book/course" to "go read your scriptures"?
