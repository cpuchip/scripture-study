# The First Book of the Chronicles

Chapter 1

The genealogies and family ties from Adam to Abraham are given—The posterity of Abraham is listed.

**1.** Adam<sup>[1a](#fn-1a)</sup>, Sheth, Enosh,

**2.** Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,

**3.** Henoch, Methuselah<sup>[3a](#fn-3a)</sup>, Lamech,

**4.** Noah, Shem<sup>[4a](#fn-4a)</sup>, Ham, and Japheth.

**5.** ¶ The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

**6.** And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.

**7.** And the sons of Javan<sup>[7a](#fn-7a)</sup>; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

**8.** ¶ The sons of Ham<sup>[8a](#fn-8a)</sup>; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

**9.** And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.

**10.** And Cush begat Nimrod<sup>[10a](#fn-10a)</sup>: he began to be mighty upon the earth.

**11.** And Mizraim<sup>[11a](#fn-11a)</sup> begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,

**12.** And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines<sup>[12a](#fn-12a)</sup>,) and Caphthorim.

**13.** And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,

**14.** The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,

**15.** And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,

**16.** And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

**17.** ¶ The sons of Shem<sup>[17a](#fn-17a)</sup>; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.

**18.** And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.

**19.** And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided<sup>[19a](#fn-19a)</sup>: and his brother’s name was Joktan.

**20.** And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,

**21.** Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,

**22.** And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,

**23.** And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.

**24.** ¶ Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,

**25.** Eber, Peleg, Reu,

**26.** Serug, Nahor, Terah<sup>[26a](#fn-26a)</sup>,

**27.** Abram; the same is Abraham<sup>[27a](#fn-27a)</sup>.

**28.** The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael<sup>[28a](#fn-28a)</sup>.

**29.** ¶ These are their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael<sup>[29a](#fn-29a)</sup>, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,

**30.** Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,

**31.** Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.

**32.** ¶ Now the sons of Keturah<sup>[32a](#fn-32a)</sup>, Abraham’s concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian<sup>[32b](#fn-32b)</sup>, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan.

**33.** And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of Keturah.

**34.** And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel.

**35.** ¶ The sons of Esau<sup>[35a](#fn-35a)</sup>; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.

**36.** The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.

**37.** The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.

**38.** And the sons of Seir<sup>[38a](#fn-38a)</sup>; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.

**39.** And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan’s sister.

**40.** The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah.

**41.** The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.

**42.** The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran.

**43.** ¶ Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of Edom<sup>[43a](#fn-43a)</sup> before any king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city was Dinhabah.

**44.** And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.

**45.** And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.

**46.** And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.

**47.** And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.

**48.** And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.

**49.** And when Shaul was dead, Baal-hanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.

**50.** And when Baal-hanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife’s name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.

**51.** ¶ Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth,

**52.** Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon,

**53.** Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar,

**54.** Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These are the dukes of Edom.
