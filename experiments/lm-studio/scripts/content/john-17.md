# John 17

🎧 [Listen to Audio](https://assets.churchofjesuschrist.org/b976dabd50aa11ed985aeeeeac1e2f501b96c2fb-64k-en.mp3)

Chapter 17

Jesus offers the great Intercessory Prayer—He is glorified by gaining eternal life—He prays for His Apostles and all the Saints—He explains how the Father and Son are one.

**1.** These words spake Jesus, and lifted up his eyes to heaven, and said, Father, the hour is come; glorify thy Son<sup>[1a](#fn-1a)</sup>, that thy Son also may glorify<sup>[1b](#fn-1b)</sup> thee:

**2.** As thou hast given him power<sup>[2a](#fn-2a)</sup> over all flesh, that he should give eternal life<sup>[2b](#fn-2b)</sup> to as many as thou hast given<sup>[2c](#fn-2c)</sup> him.

**3.** And this is life<sup>[3a](#fn-3a)</sup> eternal<sup>[3b](#fn-3b)</sup>, that they might know<sup>[3c](#fn-3c)</sup> thee the only true God<sup>[3d](#fn-3d)</sup>, and Jesus Christ, whom thou hast sent<sup>[3e](#fn-3e)</sup>.

**4.** I have glorified<sup>[4a](#fn-4a)</sup> thee on the earth: I have finished<sup>[4b](#fn-4b)</sup> the work which thou gavest me to do.

**5.** And now, O Father, glorify thou me with thine own self with the glory<sup>[5a](#fn-5a)</sup> which I had with thee before<sup>[5b](#fn-5b)</sup> the world was.

**6.** I have manifested thy name unto the men which thou gavest me out<sup>[6a](#fn-6a)</sup> of the world: thine they were, and thou gavest them me; and they have kept thy word.

**7.** Now they have known that all things whatsoever thou hast given<sup>[7a](#fn-7a)</sup> me are of thee.

**8.** For I have given unto them the words<sup>[8a](#fn-8a)</sup> which thou gavest me; and they have received them, and have known surely that I came<sup>[8b](#fn-8b)</sup> out from thee, and they have believed<sup>[8c](#fn-8c)</sup> that thou didst send me.

**9.** I pray<sup>[9a](#fn-9a)</sup> for them: I pray not for the world, but for them which thou<sup>[9b](#fn-9b)</sup> hast given me; for they are thine.

**10.** And all mine are thine, and thine are mine; and I am glorified in them.

**11.** And now I am no more in the world, but these are in the world<sup>[11a](#fn-11a)</sup>, and I come to thee. Holy Father, keep through thine own name those whom thou hast given me, that they may be one<sup>[11b](#fn-11b)</sup>, as we are.

**12.** While I was with them in the world, I kept them in thy name: those that thou gavest me I have kept, and none<sup>[12a](#fn-12a)</sup> of them is lost<sup>[12b](#fn-12b)</sup>, but the son of perdition<sup>[12c](#fn-12c)</sup>; that the scripture might be fulfilled.

**13.** And now come I to thee; and these things I speak in the world, that they might have my joy<sup>[13a](#fn-13a)</sup> fulfilled in themselves.

**14.** I have given them thy word; and the world hath hated them, because they are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.

**15.** I pray not that thou shouldest take them out of the world<sup>[15a](#fn-15a)</sup>, but that thou shouldest keep<sup>[15b](#fn-15b)</sup> them from the evil<sup>[15c](#fn-15c)</sup>.

**16.** They are not of the world<sup>[16a](#fn-16a)</sup>, even as I am not of the world.

**17.** Sanctify<sup>[17a](#fn-17a)</sup> them through thy truth<sup>[17b](#fn-17b)</sup>: thy word is truth<sup>[17c](#fn-17c)</sup>.

**18.** As thou hast sent<sup>[18a](#fn-18a)</sup> me into the world, even so have I also sent them into the world.

**19.** And for their sakes I sanctify<sup>[19a](#fn-19a)</sup> myself, that they also might be sanctified through the truth.

**20.** Neither pray<sup>[20a](#fn-20a)</sup> I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe<sup>[20b](#fn-20b)</sup> on me through their word;

**21.** That they all may be one<sup>[21a](#fn-21a)</sup>; as thou, Father<sup>[21b](#fn-21b)</sup>, art in me, and I in thee, that they also may be one<sup>[21c](#fn-21c)</sup> in us: that the world may believe that thou hast sent me.

**22.** And the glory which thou gavest me I have given them; that they may be one<sup>[22a](#fn-22a)</sup>, even as we are one<sup>[22b](#fn-22b)</sup>:

**23.** I in them, and thou in me, that they may be made perfect<sup>[23a](#fn-23a)</sup> in one; and that the world may know that thou hast sent me, and hast loved<sup>[23b](#fn-23b)</sup> them, as thou hast loved me.

**24.** Father, I will that they also, whom thou hast given me, be with me where I am; that they may behold my glory, which thou hast given me: for thou lovedst me before the foundation of the world.

**25.** O righteous Father, the world hath not known<sup>[25a](#fn-25a)</sup> thee: but I have known thee, and these have known that thou hast sent me.

**26.** And I have declared<sup>[26a](#fn-26a)</sup> unto them thy name<sup>[26b](#fn-26b)</sup>, and will declare it: that the love<sup>[26c](#fn-26c)</sup> wherewith thou hast loved<sup>[26d](#fn-26d)</sup> me may be in them, and I in them.
