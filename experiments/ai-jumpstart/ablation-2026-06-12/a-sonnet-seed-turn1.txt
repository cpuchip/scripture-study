2. **What does "done" look like for a chore?** A checkbox they click themselves? Does a parent need to confirm it?

4. **How do they access it?** Phone browser, desktop, a tablet on the fridge? That shapes how big and tappable things need to be.
5. **Who manages the chore list?** Just you as a parent, or can kids add their own?
