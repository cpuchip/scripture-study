  call; I read "keep it light / nothing fancy" as also forbidding a web framework or a
  CSS framework, not just the obvious bloat. Encoded as a covenant bound so I don't
  upgrade it later.
- **Scope fences (in intent non-goals):** no money, no approval queue yet, no streaks
  yet, no auth, no cloud. All named so a later session doesn't drift into them — three
  of them are explicit "later-phase" candidates, not permanent nos.
- **Bounds proposed, not yet ratified:** I own the project folder; I don't touch
  outside it, delete data, add dependencies, or expose beyond the LAN without asking.
  Flagged for Michael to correct.

## What was learned
