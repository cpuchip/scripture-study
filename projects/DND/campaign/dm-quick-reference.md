## Encounter Balancing (Level 1 Party of 6)

- **Easy:** 15-30 XP total (e.g., 2-3 goblins CR 1/8)
- **Medium:** 30-60 XP total (e.g., 4-6 goblins, or 1-2 CR 1/4)
- **Hard:** 60-120 XP total (e.g., 6-8 goblins, or 1 CR 1/2 + minions)
- **Deadly:** 120+ XP total (avoid at level 1)

**Adjust for:** Party has 2 tanks (Kaelen, Elara), 2 casters (Finn, Tilly), 1 hybrid caster (Malachi), 1 ranger (Lyra). Spell save DC is 13 across the board. AC ranges from 12-18.

## DM Reminders

- **Traps teach:** First trap = warning, second = test, third = punishment
- **Puzzles have failsafes:** Failure has consequences but is not lethal
- **Factions lie:** Every faction tells partial truths
- **Neverember is sympathetic:** He believes he's the hero
- **Choices are irreversible:** Two seals will fail
- **The prophecy is ambiguous:** Catalyst ≠ savior
